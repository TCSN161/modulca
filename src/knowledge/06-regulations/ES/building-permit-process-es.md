# Building Permit Process (Spain)

## Overview
Spain's building system is governed by the LOE (Building Regulation Act) and the CTE (Technical Building Code). Permits are municipal (Ayuntamiento), but technical requirements are national. Spain has a unique professional structure — the architect designs, but a separate "aparejador" (technical architect) manages construction.

## Permit Types

| Type | Applies To | Who Decides |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Licencia de obras mayor | New construction, major renovation | Ayuntamiento (municipality) |
| Licencia de obras menor | Minor works, interior renovation | Ayuntamiento (simplified) |
| Declaración responsable | Very minor works (some municipalities) | Self-declaration |

## Permit Process Steps

| Step | Action | Who | Duration |
|------|--------|-----|----------|
| 1 | Check PGOU (municipal urban plan) | Architect | 1-2 weeks |
| 2 | Proyecto básico (basic design) | Architect (colegiado) | 4-8 weeks |
| 3 | Submit licencia de obras | Architect → Ayuntamiento | — |
| 4 | Municipal review | Ayuntamiento + Colegio | 1-3 months |
| 5 | Licencia granted | Ayuntamiento | — |
| 6 | Proyecto de ejecución (detailed design) | Architect + Aparejador | Before construction |
| 7 | Construction start | Builder + Aparejador supervision | — |
| 8 | Certificate of completion | Architect + Aparejador | After construction |
| 9 | Licencia de primera ocupación | Ayuntamiento | 1-3 months |
| 10 | Escritura pública (notarial deed) | Notario | Land registry |

## Key Professionals

| Professional | Spanish Title | Role |
|-------------|-------------|------|
| Architect | Arquitecto (colegiado) | Design, basic + execution project |
| Technical architect | Aparejador / Arquitecto Técnico | Construction management, quantities |
| Structural engineer | Ingeniero de estructuras | Structural calculations |
| OCT (quality control) | Organismo de Control Técnico | Quality inspection (mandatory for insurance) |
| Notary | Notario | Deed registration |

## CTE — Código Técnico de la Edificación

| CTE Document | Coverage |
|-------------|----------|
| DB-SE | Structural safety (including seismic) |
| DB-SI | Fire safety |
| DB-SUA | Safety in use and accessibility |
| DB-HS | Health and hygiene (water, drainage) |
| DB-HR | Noise protection |
| DB-HE | Energy efficiency |

## Costs

| Item | Cost Range | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Licencia de obras | 2-4% of construction budget | Municipal tax (ICIO) |
| Architect | 5-10% of construction cost | Varies by region |
| Aparejador | 3-5% of construction cost | Mandatory |
| OCT (quality control) | 1-2% of construction cost | Required for Decenal insurance |
| Seguro decenal | 0.5-1.5% | 10-year structural insurance (like French dommage-ouvrage) |

## Key Differences

| Aspect | Spain | France | Italy | Romania |
|--------|-------|--------|-------|---------|
| Split profession | Architect + Aparejador | Architect + Bureau d'études | Architect + Ingegnere | Architect + Verificator |
| 10-year insurance | Seguro decenal (mandatory) | Dommage-ouvrage (mandatory) | No | No |
| Municipal variation | High (each Ayuntamiento) | Moderate (PLU) | High (each Comune) | Low (national) |
| Seismic risk | Moderate (south/east) | Low (most areas) | High (most areas) | High (south/east) |
| Permit cost | 2-4% of budget | Free (but taxes) | Varies | Low |
