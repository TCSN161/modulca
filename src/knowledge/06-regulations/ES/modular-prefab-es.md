# Modular & Prefab Regulations — Spain

## Overview

Spain has seen significant growth in industrialized construction (construcción industrializada). Modular buildings must comply with the same CTE requirements as traditional construction, but CE marking and DIT (Documento de Idoneidad Técnica) provide pathways for system approval.

## Regulatory Framework

### Same Standards Apply
Modular and prefab buildings in Spain must meet:
- **CTE** (all Documentos Básicos) — same as any building
- **LOE** (Ley de Ordenación de la Edificación) — building quality law
- **Building permit** (licencia de obras) — required, same process

### Certification Options

| Certification | Issuer | Purpose |
|---------------|--------|---------|
| **CE Marking** | Notified Bodies (EU) | Product conformity for construction products |
| **DIT** (Documento de Idoneidad Técnica) | ITeC / IETcc | System-level technical approval |
| **DAU** (Document d'Adequació a l'Ús) | ITeC (Catalonia) | Use adequacy document |
| **ETA** (European Technical Assessment) | EOTA | EU-wide system approval |

### DIT Process
The DIT is the standard path for innovative construction systems:
1. Manufacturer submits technical documentation
2. ITeC/IETcc evaluates — tests, calculations, inspections
3. DIT issued — valid 5 years, renewable
4. Local building control accepts DIT as proof of compliance

## Transport Regulations

| Dimension | Standard Transport | Special Permit |
|-----------|-------------------|----------------|
| Width | ≤2.55m (standard truck) | 2.55–4.50m (with escort) |
| Height | ≤4.00m | 4.00–4.50m |
| Length | ≤13.60m (semi-trailer) | Extended permits available |
| Weight | ≤24t (3-axle) / 40t (5-axle) | >40t requires special route |

ModulCA's 3×3m modules: **3.3m wide × 3.3m long × 3.2m high** (with transport frame) — standard transport, no special permits.

## Factory Inspection

- Production factory must have quality management system (ISO 9001 recommended)
- FPC (Factory Production Control) required for CE-marked products
- Periodic third-party audits for DIT/CE maintenance
