# Energy Standards — CTE DB-HE (Spain)

## Overview
Spain's energy requirements are defined in CTE DB-HE (Documento Básico de Ahorro de Energía), updated in 2019 to implement EU nZEB requirements. Spain's climate diversity — from Atlantic north to Mediterranean south to continental interior — results in different requirements per climate zone.

## Spanish Climate Zones

| Zone | Winter Severity | Summer Severity | Typical Areas |
|------|---------------|-----------------|---------------|
| A | Very mild | Variable | Canary Islands, Cadiz coast |
| B | Mild | Moderate-hot | Mediterranean coast, Andalusia |
| C | Moderate | Moderate | Northern coast (Galicia, Cantabria) |
| D | Cold | Hot | Central Spain (Madrid, Castilla) |
| E | Very cold | Variable | Mountain areas, Burgos, Ávila |

| Summer Zone | Description | Areas |
|------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Mild summer | Atlantic north |
| 2 | Moderate summer | Meseta norte, Mediterranean |
| 3 | Hot summer | Southern interior, Madrid |
| 4 | Very hot summer | Andalusia interior, Extremadura |

## CTE DB-HE Requirements (2019, nZEB)

| Parameter | Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| HE0 — Primary energy | ≤ 32-52 kWh/m²·year (non-renewable) | Depends on climate zone |
| HE1 — Thermal envelope | U-values per climate zone | Heating + cooling demand limits |
| HE2 — Installations | Minimum system efficiency | HVAC, lighting, DHW |
| HE3 — Lighting | Energy-efficient lighting | Mainly commercial |
| HE4 — Solar DHW | Mandatory solar thermal | 30-70% of DHW from solar |
| HE5 — Minimum PV | Mandatory PV for > 3,000m² | Commercial mainly |

## U-Value Requirements by Climate Zone

| Element | Zone A | Zone B | Zone C | Zone D | Zone E |
|---------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Exterior wall | 0.70 | 0.56 | 0.38 | 0.29 | 0.23 |
| Roof | 0.50 | 0.44 | 0.33 | 0.27 | 0.22 |
| Floor | 0.70 | 0.56 | 0.38 | 0.29 | 0.23 |
| Windows | 3.50 | 3.00 | 2.30 | 1.80 | 1.40 |

*Values in W/m²K*

## Solar DHW Obligation (HE4)

| Climate Zone | Minimum Solar DHW Contribution |
|-------------|-------------------------------|
| I (low radiation) | 30% |
| II | 40% |
| III | 50% |
| IV | 60% |
| V (high radiation — most of Spain) | 70% |

## ModulCA Application
- **Climate flexibility**: Same module, different insulation thickness — Zone A needs less, Zone E needs more
- **Solar advantage**: Spain's solar radiation is highest in EU — PV + solar thermal very effective
- **Solar DHW**: HE4 mandatory solar thermal is easily met with roof-mounted solar thermal panels on module roof
- **Cooling focus**: In Zone 3-4, summer comfort is critical — module overhang + cross-ventilation essential
- **Wall performance**: ModulCA's 0.18 W/m²K wall exceeds all Spanish zone requirements — universal compliance
- **Holiday home market**: Second home / vacation home market is huge in coastal Spain
