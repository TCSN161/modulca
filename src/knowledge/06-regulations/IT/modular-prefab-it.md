# Modular & Prefab Regulations — Italy

## Overview

Italy has a growing prefab market, especially in **timber construction** (costruzioni in legno) after the success of post-earthquake reconstruction using CLT and glulam. Modular buildings must comply with NTC 2018 for structural design and DPR 380/2001 for building permits — same as traditional construction.

## Regulatory Framework

### Structural Authorization
All new structures in Italy require **authorization from the Genio Civile** (Civil Engineering office) or equivalent regional body:
- Submit structural project (progetto strutturale) before construction
- Structural engineer (ingegnere strutturista) must sign
- All calculations per NTC 2018
- **Collaudo statico** (structural testing) at completion — mandatory

### Timber-Specific Requirements

| Requirement | Standard | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| CLT product standard | EN 16351 | CE marking required |
| Glulam product standard | EN 14080 | CE marking required |
| Design code | NTC 2018 Chapter 4.4 | References Eurocode 5 |
| Connections | NTC 2018 §4.4.9 | Ductility classes for seismic zones |
| Durability | UNI EN 335 | Use class + preservative treatment |
| Seismic design | NTC 2018 Chapter 7 | Behavior factor q for timber |

### CVT (Centro di Trasformazione — Processing Center)
Timber fabrication facilities must be registered as **CVT**:
- Quality control system for cutting, assembling, connecting
- Traceability of raw materials to certified source (PEFC/FSC)
- Regular testing of structural connections
- Documentation accompanies every structural element to site

## CE Marking & ETA

| Path | Scope | Timeline |
|------|-------|----------|
| **CE via harmonized standard** | Individual products (CLT, glulam) | Existing — use certified suppliers |
| **ETA (European Technical Assessment)** | Innovative systems/connections | 6–18 months to obtain |
| **CVT registration** | Factory certification | Required before production |

## Transport (Italian Roads)

| Dimension | Standard | Exceptional Transport |
|-----------|----------|---------------------|
| Width | ≤2.55m | 2.55–4.00m (with permits + escort) |
| Height | ≤4.00m | 4.00–4.50m (with permits) |
| Length | ≤13.60m (semi) | Extended available |
| Weight | ≤44t (5-axle) | Varies by route |

ModulCA modules (3.3m × 3.3m × 3.2m transport): Fit within exceptional transport category — permit required but routinely granted.

## Incentives

Italy offers substantial incentives for modular/timber construction:
- **Sismabonus**: Up to 85% tax deduction for seismic improvement (decree-dependent)
- **Ecobonus**: 50–65% for energy efficiency measures
- **Bonus Casa**: 50% for renovations (includes modular additions)
- Note: Incentive landscape changes frequently — verify current availability

## ModulCA Application

Italian market strategy for modular:
- **CVT certification**: Partner with certified Italian timber fabricator or register own facility
- **Seismic advantage**: CLT performs exceptionally well in earthquakes — major selling point in Italy
- **Post-earthquake reconstruction**: Proven market (L'Aquila 2009, Amatrice 2016) — modular timber is preferred
- **Structural documentation**: Pre-engineered calculations for each module type, ready for Genio Civile submission
- **Collaudo integration**: Testing protocol included in assembly manual
