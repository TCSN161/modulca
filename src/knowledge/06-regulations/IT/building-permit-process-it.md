# Building Permit Process (Italy)

## Overview
Italy's building system is governed by the Testo Unico dell'Edilizia (DPR 380/2001) at national level, but implemented by Comuni (municipalities) with significant regional variation. Italy has one of Europe's most complex permit processes due to the interaction of seismic regulations, landscape protection (vincolo paesaggistico), and cultural heritage constraints (Soprintendenza). A unique feature is the mandatory seismic authorization in most of the country.

## Permit Types

| Type | Italian Name | Applies To | Who Decides |
|------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Full building permit | Permesso di costruire | New construction, major renovation | Comune (municipality) |
| Certified notification | SCIA (Segnalazione Certificata di Inizio Attività) | Medium works, interior changes | Self-certified by technician |
| Light notification | CILA (Comunicazione Inizio Lavori Asseverata) | Minor works, maintenance | Self-certified |
| Free works | Edilizia libera | Very minor works (painting, flooring) | No permit needed |

## Permit Process Steps

| Step | Action | Who | Duration |
|------|--------|-----|----------|
| 1 | Check PRG/PGT (municipal urban plan) | Tecnico abilitato | 1-2 weeks |
| 2 | Progetto architettonico (architectural design) | Architect / Engineer | 4-8 weeks |
| 3 | Seismic authorization (if required) | Genio Civile (regional) | 2-6 months |
| 4 | Landscape authorization (if vincolo) | Soprintendenza | 2-4 months |
| 5 | Submit Permesso di costruire | Tecnico → Comune | — |
| 6 | Municipal review | Comune technical office | 2-4 months |
| 7 | Permesso granted | Comune | — |
| 8 | Construction start notification | Builder → Comune | Before start |
| 9 | Construction + structural testing | Direttore lavori supervision | — |
| 10 | Certificato di agibilità (habitability) | Comune | 1-3 months |
| 11 | Catasto registration | Agenzia delle Entrate | Land registry |

## Key Professionals

| Professional | Italian Title | Role |
|-------------|-------------|------|
| Architect | Architetto (iscritto all'Ordine) | Design, permits, artistic supervision |
| Engineer | Ingegnere | Structural, MEP calculations |
| Surveyor | Geometra | Small projects, cadastral work |
| Construction director | Direttore dei lavori | On-site supervision (mandatory) |
| Structural testing | Collaudatore | Structural inspection at completion |

## Unique Italian Challenges

| Challenge | Description | Impact |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Seismic authorization | Separate approval from Genio Civile — can add months | 60-80% of Italy is seismic zone |
| Soprintendenza | Cultural heritage office can block or modify designs | Applies near historic centers |
| Condono edilizio | Legacy of building amnesties — complex legal situations | Many existing buildings have irregular status |
| Regional variation | 20 regions with different implementing rules | What works in Lombardy may not work in Sicily |
| Abusivismo | History of unauthorized construction | Complicates renovation/extension permits |

## Costs

| Item | Cost Range | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Permesso di costruire | €1,000-5,000 | Oneri di urbanizzazione + costo di costruzione |
| Architect/Engineer fees | 5-12% of construction cost | Higher than most EU countries |

| Structural testing | 1-3% of structural cost | Mandatory collaudo |
| Catasto registration | €500-1,500 | Agenzia delle Entrate |
