# Energy Standards — Decreto Requisiti Minimi (Italy)

## Overview
Italy's energy performance requirements are defined by the Decreto Requisiti Minimi (DM 26/06/2015), implementing the EU EPBD. Since 2021, all new buildings must be nZEB (edifici a energia quasi zero). Italy uses 6 climate zones (A-F) based on heating degree-days, resulting in widely different requirements from Sicily to the Alps.

## Italian Climate Zones (Gradi Giorno)

| Zone | Heating Degree-Days | Typical Areas | Heating Season |
|------|-------------------|---------------|----------------|
| A | ≤ 600 GG | Lampedusa, Pantelleria | Dec 1 – Mar 15 (6h/day) |
| B | 601-900 GG | Palermo, Catania, Cagliari | Dec 1 – Mar 31 (8h/day) |
| C | 901-1400 GG | Naples, Bari, Rome coast | Nov 15 – Mar 31 (10h/day) |
| D | 1401-2100 GG | Rome, Florence, Genoa | Nov 1 – Apr 15 (12h/day) |
| E | 2101-3000 GG | Milan, Turin, Bologna, Venice | Oct 15 – Apr 15 (14h/day) |
| F | > 3000 GG | Bolzano, Belluno, mountain areas | No restriction |

## nZEB Requirements (since 2021)

| Parameter | Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| EP,gl,nren (non-renewable primary energy) | Must beat reference building | Calculated per climate zone |
| Thermal transmittance limits | U-values per climate zone | Envelope performance |
| Solar obligation | ≥ 50% DHW from renewables | Mandatory since 2017 |
| PV obligation | P ≥ 1/50 × S (surface area) | Minimum PV capacity |
| H'T (average transmittance) | Zone-dependent limits | Global envelope indicator |
| Summer performance | Asol,est/Asup,utile limits | Solar control |
| APE (energy certificate) | Mandatory for sale/rent | Classes A4 → G |

## U-Value Requirements by Climate Zone

| Element | Zone A-B | Zone C | Zone D | Zone E | Zone F |
|---------|---------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Exterior wall | 0.43 | 0.34 | 0.29 | 0.26 | 0.24 |
| Roof | 0.35 | 0.33 | 0.26 | 0.22 | 0.20 |
| Floor | 0.44 | 0.38 | 0.29 | 0.26 | 0.24 |
| Windows | 3.00 | 2.20 | 1.80 | 1.40 | 1.10 |

*Values in W/m²K — for new construction (2021+)*

## APE Energy Classes

| Class | Performance | Description |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| A4 | Best | Near-passive, very low energy |
| A3 | Excellent | High-performance nZEB |
| A2 | Very good | Standard nZEB new construction |
| A1 | Good | Minimum nZEB compliance |
| B | Above average | Good renovation |
| C | Average | Standard renovation |
| D-G | Below average → Poor | Existing stock, needs upgrade |
