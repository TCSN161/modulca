# Land Registry & Cadastre — Italy

## Overview

Italy has two parallel systems: the **Catasto** (cadastre — physical/fiscal description) and the **Conservatoria dei Registri Immobiliari** (land registry — legal ownership). Both are managed by the **Agenzia delle Entrate** (Revenue Agency).

## Catasto (Cadastre)

### Two Sections
| Type | Name | Content |
|------|------|---------|
| **Catasto Terreni** | Land cadastre | Agricultural land, unbuilt plots |
| **Catasto Fabbricati** | Building cadastre | All buildings and units |

### Key Documents
| Document | Purpose | Cost |
|----------|---------|------|
| **Visura catastale** | Shows cadastral data (owner, category, income) | Free online (own properties) |
| **Planimetria catastale** | Floor plan as registered | Free (own) / €5 (others) |
| **Mappa catastale** | Parcel boundary map | Free online (Sister portal) |
| **Estratto di mappa** | Certified parcel extract | €5 |

### Cadastral Categories (Residential)
| Category | Description | Tax Treatment |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| A/1 | Signorile (luxury) | Higher rendita |
| A/2 | Civile (standard) | ★ Most common |
| A/3 | Economica (basic) | Lower rendita |
| A/7 | Villini (small villas) | ★ ModulCA target |
| A/8 | Ville (large villas) | Higher rendita |

### Online Access
- **Sister** (sister.agenziaentrate.gov.it) — cadastral maps, free
- **Fisconline/Entratel** — visure for own properties
- **SPID** (digital identity) required for online access

## Conservatoria (Land Registry)

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Visura ipotecaria** | Ownership history, mortgages, liens |
| **Nota di trascrizione** | Registration of a transaction |
| **Ispezione ipotecaria** | Check all encumbrances on a property |

## Before Building — Checklist

1. **Visura catastale** — verify owner, category, rendita
2. **Planimetria** — confirm existing registered layout matches reality
3. **Visura ipotecaria** — check for mortgages, liens, legal disputes
4. **Certificato di Destinazione Urbanistica (CDU)** — from Comune, states buildability
5. **DOCFA update** — after construction, submit new planimetria to Catasto

## ModulCA Application

For Italy market entry:
- **Category A/7 (Villini)**: Most ModulCA homes classified here
- **Rendita catastale**: Determines IMU property tax — modular may benefit from lower rendita
- **DOCFA filing**: ModulCA provides standardized floor plans compatible with DOCFA submission
- **Cadastral maps**: Free via Sister — integrate into land analysis step
