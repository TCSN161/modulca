# Energy Standards — GEG 2024 (Germany)

## Overview
Germany replaced the EnEV (Energieeinsparverordnung) with the GEG (Gebäudeenergiegesetz — Building Energy Act) in 2020, updated significantly in 2024. The GEG mandates high energy performance and a shift to renewable heating. Germany also has the KfW Effizienzhaus system — voluntary higher standards that unlock subsidies.

## GEG 2024 Key Requirements (New Residential)

| Parameter | GEG Minimum | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Primary energy demand | ≤ 55 kWh/m²·year | Reference building method |
| U-value exterior wall | ≤ 0.24 W/m²K | DIN 4108 |
| U-value roof | ≤ 0.20 W/m²K | |
| U-value floor | ≤ 0.30 W/m²K | |
| U-value windows | ≤ 1.30 W/m²K | |
| Renewable heating | ≥ 65% renewable | Since 2024 for new buildings |
| Airtightness | Recommended n50 ≤ 1.5/h | Blower door test for calculation |
| Energy certificate | Mandatory | Energieausweis (Bedarfsausweis for new) |

## KfW Effizienzhaus Standards

| Level | Primary Energy (vs. GEG ref.) | Subsidy Available | Notes |
|-------|-------------------------------|-------------------|-------|
| KfW 55 | ≤ 55% of reference | Phased out (was standard) | Former standard |
| KfW 40 | ≤ 40% of reference | KfW loan + grant | Current best practice |
| KfW 40 Plus | ≤ 40% + energy generation | Higher subsidy | PV + battery + ventilation |
| Passivhaus | ≤ 15 kWh/m²y heating | Various programs | PHI certified |

## Heating Systems Under GEG 2024

| System | GEG 2024 Status | Notes |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| Heat pump (air/ground) | Preferred — meets 65% renewable | Most common for new builds |
| District heating (renewable) | Compliant if ≥ 65% renewable | Urban areas |
| Biomass (pellet/wood) | Compliant | Rural areas |
| Hydrogen-ready gas boiler | Transitional — only with conditions | Requires municipal heat plan |
| Gas boiler (conventional) | Not permitted in new buildings | Since 2024 |
| Oil boiler | Not permitted in new buildings | Since 2024 |

## KfW Subsidies (2024-2025)

| Program | Benefit | For |
|---------|---------|-----|
| KfW 261/262 | Up to €150,000 low-interest loan | Energy-efficient new build |
| BEG Einzelmaßnahmen | 15-45% cost subsidy | Heat pump, insulation, windows |
| Wohnungsbauprämie | €70-140/year | Low-income home savers |
| BAFA heat pump bonus | Up to 40% of costs | Replacing fossil heating |
