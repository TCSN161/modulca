# Omgevingswet — Environment and Planning Act (Netherlands)

## Overview
The Omgevingswet (Environment and Planning Act), effective January 1, 2024, replaces 26 existing laws about spatial planning, environment, nature, and construction. It is the biggest reform of Dutch environmental and planning law in decades.

## What Changed

| Before (26 laws) | After (Omgevingswet) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Wet ruimtelijke ordening (Wro) | → Omgevingswet |
| Wet milieubeheer | → Omgevingswet |
| Wet algemene bepalingen omgevingsrecht (Wabo) | → Omgevingswet |
| Bouwbesluit 2012 | → Besluit bouwwerken leefomgeving (BBL) |
| Bestemmingsplan | → Omgevingsplan |
| Omgevingsvergunning (old system) | → Omgevingsvergunning (new system) |

## Key Concepts

| Concept | What It Means | Equivalent (RO) |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Omgevingsplan** | Municipal zoning plan (replaces bestemmingsplan) | PUG + PUZ |
| **Omgevingsvergunning** | Building/planning permit | Autorizație de Construire |
| **Omgevingsvisie** | Municipal/provincial vision document | PATN / PATJ |
| **Programma** | Policy implementation programs | — |
| **Omgevingswaarden** | Environmental quality standards | — |
| **Meldingsplicht** | Notification requirement (no permit needed) | — |

## Building Activities Under Omgevingswet

| Activity | Permit Required? | Who Checks? |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| New dwelling | Yes — Omgevingsvergunning | Municipality + quality inspector |
| Small extension (< 4m) | Usually notification only | Self-certification |
| Internal modification | Usually permit-free | No check |
| Demolition | Notification | Municipality |
| Change of use | Depends on omgevingsplan | Municipality |
| Modular home (permanent) | Yes — same as traditional | Full compliance |
| Modular home (temporary) | Depends on duration | < 10 years may have relaxed rules |

## Quality Assurance — Wet kwaliteitsborging (Wkb)
Under the new system, quality checking for simple buildings (Gevolgklasse 1 — most residential) shifts from the municipality to private quality inspectors (kwaliteitsborgers).

| Aspect | Old System | New System (Wkb) |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| Who checks | Municipality (BWT) | Private quality inspector |
| When | After completion | During + after construction |
| Certification | Municipality issues permit | Inspector issues completion declaration |
| Applies to | — | Gevolgklasse 1 (simple residential) |

## ModulCA Application
For the Dutch market:
- Check the **omgevingsplan** (not bestemmingsplan) for zoning rules
- Apply for **omgevingsvergunning** via omgevingsloket.nl (digital portal)
- Hire a **kwaliteitsborger** (quality inspector) early in the process
- Factory-produced modules can be pre-inspected, potentially simplifying the Wkb process
- Modular homes are treated the same as traditional homes for permanent installation
