# Modular & Prefab Regulations (Netherlands)

## Overview
The Netherlands has a strong modular/prefab construction culture and specific regulations that address factory-built housing. The government actively promotes modular construction to address the housing shortage (woningtekort).

## Permanent vs. Temporary (Flexwonen)

| Category | Duration | Permit | Building Code |
|----------|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Permanent** | Indefinite | Full omgevingsvergunning | Full BBL compliance |
| **Tijdelijk** (temporary) | ≤ 15 years | Simplified permit | May have relaxed requirements |
| **Flexwonen** | 10-15 years | Special fast-track | Reduced BBL requirements possible |
| **Verplaatsbaar** (relocatable) | Moveable | Depends on location | Must still meet BBL at each location |

## Flexwonen (Flexible Housing)
The Dutch government promotes flexwonen to quickly address housing shortages:

| Feature | Standard Housing | Flexwonen |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| Permit process | 8+ weeks | Streamlined |
| Sound insulation | Full BBL | May be reduced |
| Energy (BENG) | Full BENG compliance | Full BENG still required |
| Location rules | Permanent zoning | Temporary zoning allowed |
| Foundation | Permanent | Removable (screw piles) |
| Duration | Indefinite | 10-15 years |

## Quality Certification for Prefab

| Certification | What It Covers | Benefit |
|--------------|---------------|---------|
| **KOMO** | Product/process quality | Recognized by building authorities |
| **SKH** | Timber products quality | For CLT, glulam components |
| **KIWA** | Product testing and certification | Third-party verified |
| **IKB** (internal) | Internal quality system | Factory QA process |
| **CE marking** | EU product compliance | Mandatory for hEN products |

## Factory Inspection and Oversight
For modular homes, the kwaliteitsborger (quality inspector) can inspect:
- **At the factory**: Verify manufacturing quality before transport
- **At the site**: Verify assembly, connections, and foundation
- This dual inspection can actually be faster than traditional construction inspection

## Government Programs Supporting Modular Construction

| Program | Purpose | Details |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Woningbouwimpuls | Subsidies for housing projects | Includes modular projects |
| Versnelling woningbouw | Speed up housing delivery | Prefab/modular is key strategy |
| Industrieel bouwen | Industrial construction programs | Government actively promotes |
| Circulair bouwen | Circular construction | Modular = demountable = circular |

## ModulCA Application for Netherlands Market
Key advantages of ModulCA in the Dutch context:
- **Housing shortage**: 900,000+ homes needed by 2030 — modular solves speed problem
- **Flexwonen**: ModulCA modules on screw piles qualify as flexwonen
- **Circular economy**: Bolted connections = fully demountable = Dutch circulair bouwen goals
- **Factory quality**: Pre-inspection reduces kwaliteitsborger time and cost
- **KOMO potential**: Factory process can be KOMO certified for Dutch market trust
- **No gas**: ModulCA heat pump heating aligns with Dutch aardgasvrij policy
