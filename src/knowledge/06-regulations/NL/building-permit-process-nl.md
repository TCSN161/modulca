# Building Permit Process (Netherlands)

## Overview
Building a new home in the Netherlands requires an Omgevingsvergunning (environmental permit) under the Omgevingswet. The process is digital, through the national portal omgevingsloket.nl.

## Process Steps

| Step | Action | Timeline | Notes |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | **Check omgevingsplan** | Before design | Municipal zoning rules |
| 2 | **Pre-consultation** (optional) | 2-4 weeks | Recommended for new builds |
| 3 | **Hire architect + engineer** | — | Licensed professionals |
| 4 | **Hire kwaliteitsborger** | Before permit | Quality inspector (Wkb) |
| 5 | **Submit omgevingsvergunning** | Via omgevingsloket.nl | Digital application |
| 6 | **Review period** | 8 weeks (regular) | 26 weeks for complex |
| 7 | **Bezwaar period** | 6 weeks | Neighbors can object |
| 8 | **Start construction** | After permit + bezwaar | Notify municipality 2 days before |
| 9 | **Kwaliteitsborger inspection** | During construction | Ongoing quality checks |
| 10 | **Gereedmelding** | After completion | Completion notification + dossier |

## Required Documents

| Document | Description | Who Prepares |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Architectural drawings | Floor plans, elevations, sections | Architect |
| Structural calculations | Eurocode compliance | Structural engineer |
| BENG calculation | Energy performance | Energy consultant |
| MPG calculation | Environmental performance | Sustainability consultant |
| Ventilation plan | Mechanical ventilation design | MEP engineer |
| Sound report | If in noise zone | Acoustic consultant |
| Construction safety plan | V&G plan | Safety coordinator |
| Borgsplan | Quality assurance plan | Kwaliteitsborger |

## Costs

| Item | Cost Range | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Leges (permit fee) | €2,000-8,000 | Percentage of construction cost |
| Architect | €5,000-15,000 | Design + documentation |
| Structural engineer | €2,000-5,000 | Calculations |
| BENG calculation | €800-1,500 | Energy performance |
| MPG calculation | €500-1,000 | Environmental performance |
| Kwaliteitsborger | €3,000-6,000 | Quality inspection |
| **Total soft costs** | **€13,000-36,000** | Before construction |

## Timeline Summary

| Phase | Duration | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Design + documentation | 2-4 months | Depends on complexity |
| Permit application + review | 2-3 months | 8 weeks standard |
| Bezwaar (objection period) | 6 weeks | After permit granted |
| Construction | 1-4 months | Modular = much faster |
| Gereedmelding | 2-4 weeks | After completion |
| **Total** | **6-12 months** | From start to move-in |

## Modular Construction Advantage
- Pre-engineered modules simplify permit documentation
- Factory quality control may streamline kwaliteitsborger process
- On-site construction phase reduced to days instead of months
- Standardized structural calculations can be reused across projects
