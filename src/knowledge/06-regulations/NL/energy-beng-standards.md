# BENG Energy Standards (Netherlands)

## Overview
BENG (Bijna Energieneutrale Gebouwen = Nearly Energy-Neutral Buildings) is the Dutch implementation of the EU EPBD nZEB requirement. Mandatory for all new buildings since January 1, 2021.

## The Three BENG Indicators

| Indicator | What It Measures | Requirement (New Woning) |
|-----------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| **BENG 1** | Maximum energy demand (heating + cooling) | ≤ 70 kWh/m² per year |
| **BENG 2** | Maximum primary fossil energy use | ≤ 50 kWh/m² per year |
| **BENG 3** | Minimum share of renewable energy | ≥ 50% |

## How to Meet BENG

### BENG 1 — Reduce Energy Demand
| Measure | Impact | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Wall insulation | Rc ≥ 4.7 m²K/W | ~180mm mineral wool |
| Roof insulation | Rc ≥ 6.3 m²K/W | ~240mm mineral wool |
| Floor insulation | Rc ≥ 3.7 m²K/W | ~140mm PIR/EPS |
| Windows | U ≤ 1.2 W/m²K | Triple glazing recommended |
| Airtightness | qv;10 ≤ 0.4 dm³/s·m² | Blower door tested |
| Thermal bridges | ψ-linear minimized | Detail design critical |

### BENG 2 — Reduce Fossil Energy
| Measure | Impact | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Heat pump | COP ≥ 3.5 | No gas connection (aardgasvrij) |
| MVHR ventilation | η ≥ 90% | Heat recovery |
| Efficient lighting | LED throughout | Low electricity demand |
| Efficient appliances | A+++ rated | Reduced plug loads |

### BENG 3 — Renewable Energy
| Measure | Contribution | Notes |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| PV solar panels | Major contributor | 8-12 panels typical |
| Solar thermal | Hot water heating | Optional |
| Heat pump (air/ground) | Counts as renewable | Primary heating |
| Green electricity contract | Does NOT count | Must be on-site generation |

## Comparison: BENG vs. Romanian nZEB vs. Passive House

| Parameter | BENG (NL) | nZEB (RO) | Passive House |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| Energy demand | ≤ 70 kWh/m²yr | ≤ 100 kWh/m²yr | ≤ 15 kWh/m²yr (heating) |
| Fossil energy | ≤ 50 kWh/m²yr | — | ≤ 120 kWh/m²yr (primary) |
| Renewables | ≥ 50% | ≥ 30% | Not specified |
| Airtightness | qv;10 ≤ 0.4 | n50 ≤ 0.6 | n50 ≤ 0.6 |
| Gas allowed? | No (aardgasvrij) | Yes | No preference |

## Aardgasvrij (Gas-Free)
Since July 2018, new Dutch homes cannot be connected to the gas network. All heating must be electric (heat pump) or district heating. This is more progressive than most EU countries.

## MPG — Environmental Performance
In addition to BENG, new Dutch homes must meet the MPG (Milieuprestatie Gebouwen):
- **MPG ≤ 0.8** (per m² GBO per year) — mandatory since 2024
- Measures embodied environmental impact (materials LCA)
- CLT/timber construction scores well on MPG
- MPG calculation per EN 15804 methodology

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA modules for the Dutch market:
- Wall Rc ≥ 4.7 (requires ~180mm insulation vs. 120mm for Romania)
- Heat pump mandatory (no gas boiler option)
- PV panels pre-designed on flat roof (8m² per module = excellent for BENG 3)
- Factory airtightness testing meets qv;10 ≤ 0.4
- CLT structure gives excellent MPG score (carbon-negative material)
