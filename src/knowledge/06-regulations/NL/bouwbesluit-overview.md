# Building Decree Overview — Bouwbesluit / BBL (Netherlands)

## Overview
The Dutch building regulations are transitioning from the **Bouwbesluit 2012** to the **Besluit bouwwerken leefomgeving (BBL)** under the new Omgevingswet (Environment and Planning Act), effective January 1, 2024.

## Key Change: Bouwbesluit 2012 → BBL 2024
The BBL replaces the Bouwbesluit but the technical requirements remain largely the same. The main change is procedural — how you apply for permits and who checks compliance.

## Structure of the BBL

| Chapter | Topic | Key Requirements |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| 2 | Safety | Structural, fire safety, use safety |
| 3 | Health | Ventilation, daylight, moisture, noise |
| 4 | Usability | Room sizes, accessibility, toilets |
| 5 | Energy efficiency | BENG standards, insulation |
| 6 | Environment | Sustainability, MPG calculation |
| 7 | Installation | Electrical, plumbing, heating |

## Key Requirements for Residential Buildings (Woning)

### Structural
| Parameter | Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Design life | 50 years (reference period) | Eurocode NEN-EN 1990 |
| Consequence class | CC2 | Standard residential |
| Foundation | Below frost line (0.80m) | Pile foundations common in west NL |

### Room Sizes (BBL Chapter 4)
| Room | Minimum | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Verblijfsruimte (habitable room) | 5.0 m² min | With min 1.80m width |
| Verblijfsgebied (living zone) | 24 m² total | Per dwelling unit |
| Ceiling height | ≥ 2.60m (new build) | ≥ 2.40m existing |
| Kitchen | Part of verblijfsgebied | No separate minimum |
| Toilet | Required per dwelling | Min 0.90 × 1.20m |
| Bathroom | Min 1.60 m² | With shower or bath |

### Ventilation (BBL Chapter 3)
| Space | Requirement | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Living room | 0.9 dm³/s per m² | Minimum ventilation |
| Bedroom | 0.9 dm³/s per m² | Or window |
| Kitchen | 21 dm³/s extraction | Mechanical |
| Bathroom | 14 dm³/s extraction | Mechanical |
| Toilet | 7 dm³/s extraction | Mechanical |

### Sound Insulation
| Element | Requirement | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|
| Between dwellings (airborne) | ≥ 52 dB | DnT;A;k |
| Between dwellings (impact) | ≤ 54 dB | LnT;A;k |
| From outside | Depends on noise zone | Geluidbelasting |

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA modules designed for the Dutch market must meet BBL requirements, which differ from Romanian standards in several areas:
- Higher sound insulation between units (52 dB vs. 45 dB)
- BENG energy standards instead of C107
- Different foundation requirements (piling common in western NL)
- MPG (environmental performance) calculation required
