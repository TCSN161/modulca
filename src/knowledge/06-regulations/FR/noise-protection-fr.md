# Noise & Acoustic Regulations (France)

## Overview
France's acoustic regulations for new housing are defined by the Arrêté du 30 juin 1999 (NRA — Nouvelle Réglementation Acoustique). The NRA sets minimum sound insulation performance for airborne noise, impact noise, equipment noise, and exterior noise protection. These apply to all new dwellings, including modular and prefabricated construction, and compliance is verified through post-construction acoustic testing.

## Regulatory Framework

| Text | Scope |
|------|-------|
| Arrêté du 30 juin 1999 | Acoustic performance of new housing |
| Code de la Construction (Art. R.111-4 et seq.) | General acoustic requirements |
| NF EN ISO 16283 series | In-situ measurement methods |
| NF EN ISO 717-1 and 717-2 | Rating of sound insulation (Rw, Ln,w) |
| Arrêté du 30 mai 1996 | Noise from classified facilities (ICPE) |
| Arrêté du 23 juillet 2013 | Noise attestation requirements |

## NRA Requirements — Individual Houses (Maisons Individuelles)

For detached single-family homes, the NRA requirements are minimal:

| Parameter | Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Airborne noise between rooms | No specific requirement | Within same dwelling |
| Facade insulation (DnT,A,tr) | ≥ 30 dB (standard), ≥ 35-45 dB (noisy zones) | See exterior noise classification |
| Equipment noise (heating, ventilation) | See equipment limits below | Applies to all houses |
| Garage-to-dwelling insulation | DnT,A ≥ 53 dB | If attached garage |

### Semi-Detached / Grouped Houses (Maisons Jumelées/Groupées)

| Parameter | Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Airborne between dwellings | DnT,A ≥ 53 dB | Party wall |
| Impact between dwellings | L'nT,w ≤ 58 dB | If shared floor/ceiling |
| Facade insulation | ≥ 30 dB (standard zone) | Same as individual |

## NRA Requirements — Collective Housing (Logements Collectifs)

| Parameter | Indicator | Requirement |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Airborne noise between dwellings | DnT,A | ≥ 53 dB |
| Airborne from circulation to dwelling | DnT,A | ≥ 40 dB |
| Airborne from garage to dwelling | DnT,A | ≥ 55 dB |
| Impact noise between dwellings | L'nT,w | ≤ 58 dB |
| Impact from circulation to dwelling | L'nT,w | ≤ 58 dB |
| Reverberation in common areas | Tr | ≤ 1.2 s (stairwells), ≤ 1.8 s (parking) |

## Equipment Noise Limits

| Equipment | In the Dwelling | From Adjacent Dwelling |
|-----------|----------------|----------------------|
| Individual heating (boiler, heat pump) | LnAT ≤ 35 dB(A) continuous | LnAT ≤ 30 dB(A) |
| VMC (mechanical ventilation) | LnAT ≤ 30 dB(A) in living rooms, ≤ 35 dB(A) in kitchen | Same limits for neighbor |
| Collective equipment (lift, pipes) | — | LnAT ≤ 30 dB(A) continuous, ≤ 35 dB(A) intermittent |
| Water pipes, flushing | — | LnAT ≤ 35 dB(A) peak |

## Exterior Noise Protection — Facade Insulation

Facade insulation requirements depend on the site's exposure to transport noise:

### Noise Exposure Classification

| Infrastructure Noise Category | DnT,A,tr Required | Typical Context |
|------------------------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| No classified road nearby | ≥ 30 dB | Rural, quiet suburban |
| Category 5 | ≥ 30 dB | Minor local roads |
| Category 4 | ≥ 32 dB | Busy local roads |
| Category 3 | ≥ 35 dB | Departmental roads, moderate traffic |
| Category 2 | ≥ 38 dB | Major roads, railways |
| Category 1 | ≥ 42-45 dB | Highways, high-speed rail, airports |

Check noise classifications on the préfecture website or the PLU annexes for each commune.

## Acoustic Performance of Typical Constructions

| Construction | Rw (lab value) | Notes |
|-------------|---------------|-------|
| CLT 100mm (3-layer) | ~33 dB | Bare, without finishing |
| CLT 100mm + 50mm mineral wool + plasterboard | ~50-54 dB | Standard lining |
| CLT 160mm + double lining (resilient channels) | ~58-62 dB | Party wall specification |
| Double CLT wall with cavity (2 x 100mm + 50mm gap + wool) | ~60-65 dB | Excellent for party walls |
| Timber floor (CLT 140mm + floating screed) | L'nT,w ~55-60 dB | Impact noise |
| Timber floor (CLT + resilient layer + 50mm screed) | L'nT,w ~48-55 dB | Good impact performance |

## Acoustic Attestation (Since 2013)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| When required | New collective housing (> 10 dwellings, or in noise-classified zone) |
| Who provides | Acousticien qualifié (qualified acoustician) |
| When | Two stages: design attestation (with PC) + completion attestation (with DAACT) |
| Testing | In-situ measurements per NF EN ISO 16283 on sample dwellings |
| Not required for | Individual houses (but recommended) |

## Common Acoustic Pitfalls in Modular/Timber Construction

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Low-frequency airborne transmission | Lightweight walls vibrate easily at low frequencies | Add mass (double plasterboard, concrete screed) |
| Flanking transmission | Sound travels through junctions between modules | Resilient junction details, acoustic strips |
| Impact noise through floors | Direct connection of floor to structure | Floating floor with resilient layer (≥ 10mm) |
| Module-to-module gaps | Poor sealing at module joints | Acoustic sealant, compressed mineral wool gaskets |
| Plumbing noise | Rigid pipe connections to structure | Acoustic pipe clamps, resilient mounts |

## ModulCA Application
- **Party wall strategy**: For semi-detached ModulCA homes, a double-CLT wall with cavity achieves DnT,A ≥ 53 dB
- **Floating floors**: Factory-installed floating screed on resilient layer for impact noise compliance
- **Facade insulation**: CLT + insulation + cladding systems reach DnT,A,tr ≥ 35 dB for most zones
- **VMC integration**: Ventilation ducts acoustically treated in factory — meets ≤ 30 dB(A) in living rooms
- **Module junction sealing**: Proprietary acoustic gaskets at module-to-module connections prevent flanking paths
- **Heat pump placement**: External unit positioned per acoustic study to meet 30 dB(A) neighbor limit
