# Accessibility Requirements (France)

## Overview
France's accessibility framework is anchored in the Loi n° 2005-102 du 11 février 2005 (Loi Handicap), one of Europe's most comprehensive disability laws. It covers all types of disability — motor, visual, auditory, cognitive, and psychological. For new housing (logements neufs), specific requirements ensure that dwellings are either accessible or adaptable for persons with reduced mobility (PMR).

## Regulatory Framework

| Text | Scope | Key Content |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Loi n° 2005-102 (11 Feb 2005) | All buildings | Universal accessibility principle |
| Décret n° 2006-555 | New housing | Technical requirements for dwellings |
| Arrêté du 24 décembre 2015 | New housing | Detailed dimensional requirements |
| Arrêté du 20 avril 2017 | Existing ERP | Accessibility agenda for public buildings |
| Code de la Construction (R.111-18 to R.111-18-10) | All construction | Codified accessibility rules |

## Requirements for New Housing (Logements Neufs)

### Individual Houses (Maisons Individuelles)

Since the 2015 reform, new individual houses must be **adaptable** rather than fully accessible from construction:

| Element | Requirement | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|
| Entrance | At least one accessible entrance (no step, or ramp ≤ 5%) | Level threshold or max 2cm seuil |
| Ground floor | One habitable room + kitchen + WC on ground floor | Can serve as bedroom if needed |
| Ground floor WC | Min 1.30m x 0.80m usable | Adaptable for wheelchair later |
| Doorways (ground floor) | ≥ 0.83m clear passage (0.90m door leaf) | All ground floor doors |
| Corridors (ground floor) | ≥ 0.90m wide | Wheelchair passage |
| Parking | 1 adapted space if private parking | 3.30m wide minimum |
| Outdoor path | ≥ 1.20m wide, firm surface, ≤ 5% slope | From parking/street to entrance |

### Collective Housing (Logements Collectifs)

| Element | Requirement | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|
| Common areas | Fully wheelchair accessible | Halls, corridors, lifts |
| Lift | Mandatory if building ≥ R+3 (4 floors) | Min 1.00m x 1.30m cabin |
| Unit interior | All rooms on entry level accessible | Including bathroom, WC, kitchen |
| Bathroom | Espace de manoeuvre 1.50m turning circle | Or adaptable with removable partition |
| Balcony/terrace | Accessible from living area | Max 2cm threshold |

## Key Dimensional Requirements (Arrêté 24 Dec 2015)

| Dimension | Individual House | Collective Housing |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Exterior path width | ≥ 1.20m | ≥ 1.40m |
| Door clear passage | ≥ 0.83m | ≥ 0.83m |
| Corridor width | ≥ 0.90m | ≥ 1.20m (common), ≥ 0.90m (unit) |
| WC minimum area | 1.30m x 0.80m | 0.80m x 1.30m + lateral transfer space |
| Turning circle (wheelchair) | Recommended 1.50m | Required 1.50m in at least one room |
| Ramp max slope | 5% (max 10m), 8% (max 2m), 12% (max 0.50m) | Same |
| Step max height (seuil) | 2cm | 2cm |
| Kitchen aisle | ≥ 1.50m between counters | ≥ 1.50m |

## Adaptable vs. Accessible

France distinguishes between:

| Concept | Definition | Applies To |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Accessible | Immediately usable by PMR without modification | Collective common areas, ERP |
| Adaptable | Can be made accessible with simple works (no structural changes) | Individual house interiors, some collective unit features |
| Évolutif (since 2018 Loi ELAN) | 20% of units in collective buildings must be fully accessible; rest must be évolutif | New collective housing (≥ 2019) |

The Loi ELAN (2018) relaxed collective housing rules: only 20% of units must be immediately accessible (previously 100%), but all others must be "évolutive" — convertible with minor works.

## ERP (Établissements Recevant du Public)

If a ModulCA building serves a public function:

| ERP Category | Capacity | Accessibility Deadline |
|-------------|----------|----------------------|
| 1st category | > 1,500 people | Fully accessible (Ad'AP completed) |
| 2nd category | 701-1,500 | Fully accessible |
| 3rd category | 301-700 | Fully accessible |
| 4th category | ≤ 300 (except 5th) | Fully accessible |
| 5th category | Below thresholds | At least one accessible area per service |

## Sanctions and Control

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Attestation required | Accessibility attestation at completion (DAACT) for buildings > R+2 or collective housing |
| Issued by | Contrôleur technique or architecte |
| Sanctions (non-compliance) | Up to 45,000 EUR fine, 6 months imprisonment (for ERP) |
| Derogation possible | Technical impossibility, conservation of heritage, disproportionate cost (requires prefect approval) |

## ModulCA Application
- **Ground-floor adaptable**: ModulCA single-story and two-story designs include adaptable ground floor layout meeting the 2015 Arrêté
- **Factory precision for thresholds**: Level thresholds (≤ 2cm) between modules are factory-controlled, avoiding on-site errors
- **Wide corridors by default**: Module interior designs use 0.90m+ corridors and 0.83m clear door openings as standard
- **Single-story option**: ModulCA plain-pied (single-story) models are inherently more accessible — no stairs needed
- **Adaptable bathroom**: Ground floor bathroom designed with future lateral transfer space in mind
