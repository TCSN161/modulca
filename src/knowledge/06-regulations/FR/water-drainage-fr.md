# Water & Drainage Regulations (France)

## Overview
French water and drainage regulations cover three domains: drinking water supply (eau potable), wastewater treatment (assainissement), and stormwater management (eaux pluviales). The system distinguishes between collective (public sewer) and individual (autonomous) wastewater treatment. Every new construction must address all three, and the requirements significantly affect site planning for modular homes.

## Regulatory Framework

| Text | Scope |
|------|-------|
| Code de la Santé Publique (Art. L.1331-1 et seq.) | Mandatory sewer connection, drinking water |
| Loi sur l'Eau (n° 2006-1772) | Water resource protection framework |
| Code de l'Environnement (Art. L.214-1 et seq.) | Water use, stormwater regulation |
| Arrêté du 7 septembre 2009 | Technical standards for individual sanitation (ANC) |
| Arrêté du 21 juillet 2015 | Update to individual sanitation standards |
| PLU (Articles 4) | Local rules for utility connections |

## Drinking Water (Eau Potable)

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|------------|
| Connection | Mandatory to public network if available |
| Minimum pressure | 2 bar at entry point |
| Backflow prevention | Disconnecteur or clapet anti-retour required |
| Meter location | At property boundary (regard de compteur) |
| Private well | Déclaration en mairie required; cannot substitute public supply |
| Water quality | Must meet Directive 98/83/CE transposed standards |

### Connection Process

| Step | Action | Who |
|------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Request raccordement to water utility | Owner via mairie or syndicat des eaux |
| 2 | Utility designs the connection | Water operator |
| 3 | Installation of branchement and meter | Water operator |
| 4 | Internal plumbing (NF DTU 60.1) | Plumber |
| 5 | Conformity check | Water operator |

## Wastewater — Assainissement Collectif (Public Sewer)

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|------------|
| Obligation | Mandatory connection within 2 years if public sewer exists (Art. L.1331-1 CSP) |
| System type | Séparatif (separate stormwater/wastewater) or unitaire (combined) — depends on commune |
| Connection point | Regard de branchement at property boundary |
| Wastewater only | Eaux usées (grey + black water) — no stormwater in séparatif systems |
| Grease trap | Required for kitchens in some communes |
| Industrial water | Pre-treatment may be required (convention spéciale) |

### Fees

| Fee | Typical Range | Notes |
|-----|--------------|-------|
| Participation au raccordement (PRE) | 2,000-6,000 EUR | One-time connection fee |
| Redevance assainissement | 1.50-3.00 EUR/m³ | Ongoing, based on water consumption |

## Wastewater — Assainissement Non Collectif (ANC / Individual)

When no public sewer is available, an individual treatment system (ANC) is required:

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|------------|
| Regulatory basis | Arrêté du 7 septembre 2009 (modified 2015) |
| Control authority | SPANC (Service Public d'Assainissement Non Collectif) |
| Design basis | Number of pièces principales (main rooms), not occupants |
| Minimum capacity | 5 EH (Équivalents-Habitants) for ≤ 5 pièces principales |
| Soil study | Étude de sol (percolation test) mandatory |
| SPANC approval | Required BEFORE construction (avis favorable) |

### ANC System Options

| System | Description | Footprint | Suitability |
|--------|------------|-----------|-------------|
| Filière traditionnelle (tranchées d'épandage) | Gravel trenches + soil infiltration | 60-150 m² | Permeable soils, large plots |
| Filtre à sable | Sand filter (vertical or horizontal) | 25-50 m² | Less permeable soils |
| Micro-station d'épuration | Compact biological treatment | 5-10 m² | Small plots, all soils |
| Filtre compact | Compact media filter (coco, zeolite) | 10-20 m² | Medium plots |
| Filtre planté (phytoépuration) | Constructed wetland with reeds | 20-40 m² | Eco-projects, sufficient space |

### SPANC Process

| Step | Action | When |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Étude de sol et de filière | Before permit application |
| 2 | Submit ANC project to SPANC | With building permit |
| 3 | SPANC issues avis | Within 1 month |
| 4 | Install system | During construction |
| 5 | SPANC conformity inspection | Before backfilling |
| 6 | Periodic inspection | Every 4-10 years |

## Stormwater Management (Eaux Pluviales)

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|------------|
| General principle | Owner manages stormwater on own property (Art. 641 Code Civil) |
| PLU rules | Many PLUs require on-site retention/infiltration |
| Infiltration preferred | Infiltrate on-site if soil permits |
| Discharge to public system | Only with municipal authorization |
| Retention requirements | Typically 30-100 L/m² of impervious surface (varies by commune) |
| Flood zones | PPRi may impose stricter limits |

### Common Stormwater Solutions

| Solution | Capacity | Cost Range | Best For |
|----------|----------|------------|----------|
| Puisard (soakaway) | 1-5 m³ | 500-2,000 EUR | Permeable soils |
| Cuve de rétention enterrée | 2-20 m³ | 2,000-8,000 EUR | Impermeable soils, reuse |
| Noue paysagère (swale) | Variable | 1,000-3,000 EUR | Landscape integration |
| Toiture végétalisée (green roof) | 30-50 L/m² retention | 60-120 EUR/m² | Urban, flat roofs |
| Tranchée drainante | 1-3 m³/linear meter | 80-150 EUR/m | Along buildings |

## Key Distances and Setbacks

| Element | Minimum Distance | From |
|---------|-----------------|------|
| ANC system | 5 m | From building |
| ANC system | 3 m | From property boundary |
| ANC system | 35 m | From well/water source |
| ANC infiltration | 3 m | From trees |
| Stormwater infiltration | 5 m | From building foundations |
| Drinking water pipe | 0.30 m vertical | From sewer pipe (above) |

## ModulCA Application
- **Pre-planned connections**: Module designs include pre-positioned plumbing stubs for rapid raccordement
- **Séparatif by default**: Separate grey/black water and stormwater outlets built into modules
- **ANC compatible**: For rural plots, foundation plans account for ANC setback distances
- **Compact footprint advantage**: Smaller building footprint = less impervious surface = easier stormwater compliance
- **Rainwater harvesting ready**: Optional cuve de récupération integrates with modular roof drainage system
