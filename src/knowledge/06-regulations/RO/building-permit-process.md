# Building Permit Process (Romania)

## Overview
Steps and documents required for Autorizație de Construire in Romania.

## Process Steps

| Step | Description | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| Certificat de Urbanism | Step 1 — Urban planning certificate | From local primărie, 30 days |
| Studiu geotehnic | Required geotechnical survey | Soil investigation for foundations |
| Proiect tehnic (DTAC) | Required technical project | Architect-signed drawings |
| Avize utilități | Required utility approvals | Water, electric, gas, telecom |
| Autorizație de Construire | Step 2 — Building permit | Valid 12 months (extendable 12) |
| Diriginte de șantier | Required site supervisor | Licensed construction supervisor |
| Recepție la terminare | Step 3 — Final inspection | Completion inspection + cadastre update |
| Intabulare | Step 4 — Land registry | Registration with ANCPI |
| Timeline estimate | 3–6 months | From CU to AC, normal flow |

## Modular Construction Advantage
Factory-built modules can be pre-certified with standard technical documentation, reducing the project-specific DTAC complexity. The diriginte de șantier role is simplified for modular assembly compared to traditional on-site construction.
