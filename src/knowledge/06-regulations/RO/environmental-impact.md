# Environmental Impact Assessment (Romania)

## Overview
Certain construction projects in Romania require an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) or at minimum an environmental screening from the local APM (Agenția pentru Protecția Mediului).

## When Required

| Project Type | EIA Level | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Single-family home (< 500m²) | Screening only | Acord de mediu simplu |
| Residential development (> 10 units) | Full EIA possible | Depends on location |
| Near protected areas (Natura 2000) | Always assessed | Additional biodiversity study |
| Near watercourses | Assessed | Water management plan |
| Industrial/commercial > 1000m² | Full EIA | Mandatory |
| Rural, no protected areas | Usually exempt | Notification only |

## Process

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Submit notification to APM | With CU application |
| 2 | APM screening decision | 15–30 days |
| 3 | If required: prepare environmental report | 1–3 months |
| 4 | Public consultation | 30 days |
| 5 | APM issues Acord de Mediu | Required for AC |

## Acord de Mediu (Environmental Permit)

| Aspect | What's Assessed | Notes |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| Air quality | Dust during construction, heating emissions | Usually minor for residential |
| Noise | Construction noise, operational noise | Limited hours: 07:00–22:00 |
| Water | Wastewater discharge, drainage impact | Connection or treatment plan |
| Waste | Construction waste management | Segregation and disposal plan |
| Biodiversity | Protected species, habitats | Critical near Natura 2000 |
| Landscape | Visual impact | Important in scenic areas |

## ModulCA Application
Modular construction significantly reduces environmental impact:
- **Shorter site works** — less noise, dust, and traffic disruption
- **Less construction waste** — factory cutting optimizes materials (waste < 5% vs. 15-20% traditional)
- **No wet trades on site** — no concrete mixing, painting, plastering
- **Reversible foundations** — screw piles can be removed, restoring the site

These advantages should be highlighted in the APM notification to expedite screening.
