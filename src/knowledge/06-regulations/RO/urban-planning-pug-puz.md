# Urban Planning — PUG, PUZ, PUD (Romania)

## Overview
Romanian urban planning operates through a hierarchy of plans that determine what can be built where. Understanding these is essential before purchasing land or designing.

## Planning Hierarchy

| Plan | Full Name | Level | What It Defines |
|------|-----------|-------|-----------------|
| PATN | Planul de Amenajare a Teritoriului Național | National | National infrastructure, protected zones |
| PATJ | Planul de Amenajare a Teritoriului Județean | County | County-level strategic planning |
| PUG | Planul Urbanistic General | Municipal | City-wide zoning, land use, building regulations |
| PUZ | Planul Urbanistic Zonal | Zonal | Detailed zoning for specific areas |
| PUD | Planul Urbanistic de Detaliu | Plot | Specific plot buildability, setbacks, heights |

## Key Zoning Parameters

| Parameter | Abbreviation | Typical Values | What It Means |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| Building coverage | POT | 35–45% | Max footprint as % of lot area |
| Land use coefficient | CUT | 0.9–1.5 | Max total built area / lot area |
| Building height | Hmax | 7–10m residential | Maximum height (to cornice) |
| Number of floors | Regim de înălțime | P+1, P+1+M | Ground + floors + attic |
| Front setback | Retragere frontală | 3–5m | From street boundary |
| Side setback | Retragere laterală | ≥ 1.0m | From side boundaries |
| Rear setback | Retragere posterioară | ≥ 2.0m | From rear boundary |
| Green space | Spațiu verde | 20–30% | Required landscaped area |

## When a PUZ/PUD is Needed
- **PUZ required**: When proposed use differs from PUG zoning, or plot > 1000m² without detailed zoning
- **PUD required**: When specific plot parameters need defining (setbacks, height, orientation)
- **Timeline**: PUZ approval takes 3-6 months, PUD takes 2-4 months
- **Cost**: PUZ: €3,000-10,000 | PUD: €1,500-5,000

## Certificat de Urbanism (CU)
The CU (Urban Planning Certificate) is the first document needed — it tells you:
- What zone the plot is in
- What POT/CUT/height rules apply
- What utilities and approvals you need
- Whether a PUZ/PUD is required
