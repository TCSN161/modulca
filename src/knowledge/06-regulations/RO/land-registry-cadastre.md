# Land Registry & Cadastre (Romania)

## Overview
All land and buildings in Romania must be registered in the National Cadastre and Land Registry (Cartea Funciară), administered by ANCPI (Agenția Națională de Cadastru și Publicitate Imobiliară).

## Registration Process

| Step | Action | Timeline | Cost |
|------|--------|----------|------|
| 1 | Hire authorized topographer | — | €500–1,500 |
| 2 | Cadastral survey (ridicare topografică) | 1–2 weeks | Included above |
| 3 | Prepare cadastral documentation | 1 week | — |
| 4 | Submit to OCPI (local cadastre office) | — | €0–500 (systematic registration is free) |
| 5 | OCPI verification and registration | 15–30 days | — |
| 6 | Receive Extras de Carte Funciară | — | Proof of ownership |

## Key Documents

| Document | Purpose | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Extras de Carte Funciară | Ownership proof | Required for all transactions |
| Plan de amplasament | Site plan | Shows boundaries, area |
| Plan de releveu | Floor plan | For registered buildings |
| Certificat fiscal | Tax clearance | From local primărie |

## After Construction
After completing a building and receiving Recepție la Terminare (completion certificate):
1. Update cadastral documentation with new building
2. Submit Releveu (floor plan) to OCPI
3. Intabulare (registration) of the building on the land title
4. Receive updated Extras de Carte Funciară

## Systematic Registration Program
Romania is conducting a national systematic registration program (free for owners) — check if your area is covered at ancpi.ro.

## ModulCA Application
Modular buildings follow the same intabulare process as traditional construction. The building's footprint and floor plan (releveu) are submitted after the Recepție la Terminare inspection. The modular nature doesn't affect the registration process.
