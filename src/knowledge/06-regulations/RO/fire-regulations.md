# Fire Safety Regulations (Romania)

## Overview
Romanian fire safety requirements for residential buildings, governed by P118/1999 and enforced by ISU (Inspectoratul pentru Situații de Urgență).

## Fire Resistance Requirements

| Element | Residential (1-2 floors) | Multi-storey (3+) |
|---------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Load-bearing walls | REI 30 | REI 60 |
| Floors between storeys | REI 30 | REI 60 |
| Roof structure | RE 15 | RE 30 |
| Partition walls | EI 15 | EI 30 |
| Staircase walls | REI 60 | REI 90 |

## Detection & Protection

| Requirement | Standard | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Smoke detectors | Each bedroom + hallway | Optical type, EN 14604 |
| CO detector | Near gas appliances | If gas is used |
| Fire extinguisher | 1 per dwelling, ABC 6kg | Recommended |
| Fire blanket | Kitchen | Recommended |
| Emergency lighting | Escape routes | Battery backup 1 hour min |
| ISU aviz | Required for AC | Fire safety approval from ISU |

## Escape Routes

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Max travel distance | 20m (single direction) | To exit door |
| Max travel distance | 40m (two directions) | Alternative exits |
| Exit door width | ≥ 800mm | Clear opening |
| Exit door swing | Outward | Direction of escape |
| Corridor min width | 1.20m | On escape routes |

## Cladding & Materials

| Requirement | Class | Notes |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| External cladding | Min C-s2, d0 | B-s2, d0 for buildings >28m |
| Insulation (in wall) | Min E | D or better recommended |
| Internal lining | Min D-s2, d0 | Escape routes: A2-s1, d0 |
| Roof covering | BROOF(t1) | External fire exposure |

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA modules are designed to REI 60 standard (exceeds P118 requirement for 1-2 storey residential). ISU aviz (fire safety approval) is obtained as part of the building permit process — modular construction with factory-certified fire ratings simplifies this approval.
