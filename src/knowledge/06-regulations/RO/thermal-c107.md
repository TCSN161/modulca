# Thermal Regulations (C107)

## Overview
Romanian thermal performance code for residential buildings.

## Climate Zones

| Zone | Design Ext. Temperature | Coverage |
|------|------------------------|----------|
| Zone I (south) | Te = -15°C | Southern plains |
| Zone II (center) | Te = -18°C | Transylvania |
| Zone III (mountain) | Te = -21°C | Mountain areas |
| Interior design temp. | 20°C | Living spaces |

## Requirements

| Parameter | Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Condensation check | Required | Glaser method or EN 13788 |
| Summer overheating | Ti ≤ 27°C | Without active cooling |
| Energy certificate | Mandatory | Before sale or rent |

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA modules are designed to exceed C107 requirements across all three climate zones. The factory-controlled wall assembly ensures consistent thermal performance regardless of on-site conditions.
