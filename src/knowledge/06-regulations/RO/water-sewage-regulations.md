# Water Supply & Sewage Regulations (Romania)

## Overview
Water supply and wastewater discharge in Romania are regulated by Legea Apei (Water Law) and local utility operators. Rural areas often require private solutions (wells, septic systems).

## Water Supply Options

| Source | When Used | Approval Needed | Notes |
|--------|----------|----------------|-------|
| Public network | Urban/suburban | Aviz Apă Canal | Connection contract |
| Private well (puț forat) | Rural, no network | ABADL permit for >50m depth | Water quality testing required |
| Rainwater harvesting | Supplementary | None for garden use | Not potable without treatment |

## Public Water Connection

| Parameter | Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Connection pipe | PE-HD, DN 32–50mm | To public main |
| Water meter | Required | Installed by operator |
| Backflow prevention | Required | At property boundary |
| Min. pressure | 1.5 bar | At meter |
| Design consumption | 120–150 l/person/day | Romanian standard |
| Fire hydrant | If >250m from existing | May be required by ISU |

## Sewage & Wastewater

| Option | When Used | Capacity | Notes |
|--------|----------|----------|-------|
| Public sewer | Urban/suburban | — | Connection contract required |
| Septic tank (fosa septică) | Rural, no sewer | 2,000–5,000 l | Periodic emptying (vidanjare) |
| Wastewater treatment plant | Rural, eco-option | 4–8 PE | EN 12566 certified micro-plant |
| Grey water recycling | Supplementary | — | For garden irrigation |

## Wastewater Discharge Standards

| Parameter | NTPA 002 Limit | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| BOD₅ | ≤ 25 mg/l | Into public sewer |
| Suspended solids | ≤ 35 mg/l | — |
| pH | 6.5–8.5 | — |
| Temperature | ≤ 40°C | At discharge point |

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA wet modules (kitchen, bathroom) have pre-installed plumbing with standard connection points at the module edge. For rural sites without public sewer, a micro wastewater treatment plant (EN 12566) is recommended over a traditional septic tank.
