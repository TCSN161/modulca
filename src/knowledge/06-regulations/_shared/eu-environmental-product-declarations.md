# Environmental Product Declarations (EPD)

## Overview
EPDs provide standardised, third-party verified environmental data for construction products based on Life Cycle Assessment (LCA). Increasingly required for green building certifications and public procurement.

## What an EPD Contains

| Module | Phase | Covers |
|--------|-------|--------|
| A1-A3 | Product stage | Raw materials, transport, manufacturing |
| A4-A5 | Construction | Transport to site, installation |
| B1-B7 | Use stage | Maintenance, repair, replacement, energy/water use |
| C1-C4 | End of life | Demolition, transport, waste, disposal |
| D | Beyond lifecycle | Reuse, recovery, recycling potential |

## Key Environmental Indicators

| Indicator | Unit | What It Measures |
|-----------|------|-----------------|
| GWP | kg CO₂-eq | Global Warming Potential (carbon footprint) |
| ODP | kg CFC-11-eq | Ozone Depletion Potential |
| AP | kg SO₂-eq | Acidification Potential |
| EP | kg PO₄-eq | Eutrophication Potential |
| PENRT | MJ | Non-renewable primary energy |
| PERT | MJ | Renewable primary energy |

## EPD Data for Common Modular Materials

| Material | GWP (A1-A3) | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| CLT (per m³) | -500 to -700 kg CO₂-eq | Carbon negative (sequestered) |
| Glulam (per m³) | -450 to -600 kg CO₂-eq | Carbon negative |
| Steel (per t) | +1,500 to +2,000 kg CO₂-eq | High embodied carbon |
| Concrete (per m³) | +200 to +400 kg CO₂-eq | Cement production |
| Mineral wool (per m³) | +30 to +80 kg CO₂-eq | Moderate impact |
| EPS insulation (per m³) | +60 to +120 kg CO₂-eq | Petrochemical-based |

## ModulCA Application
CLT-based ModulCA modules have inherently low (negative) embodied carbon. EPD data can be aggregated to provide a whole-building LCA, which is increasingly required for:
- BREEAM / LEED / DGNB certification
- Dutch MPG (Environmental Performance of Buildings) calculation
- French RE2020 carbon budget compliance
- EU taxonomy for sustainable finance
