# EU Construction Products Regulation (CPR)

## Overview
The CPR establishes harmonised conditions for the marketing of construction products in the EU. Products covered by harmonised European standards (hEN) must carry CE marking.

## Key Requirements

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| CE marking | Mandatory for products covered by hEN |
| Declaration of Performance (DoP) | Manufacturer must declare product performance |
| Basic requirements | Mechanical strength, fire safety, hygiene, safety in use, noise, energy, sustainability |
| Notified bodies | Third-party assessment for certain product categories |

## Relevant Product Standards for Modular Construction

| Product | Standard | Key Properties |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| CLT panels | EN 16351 | Mechanical, fire, thermal |
| Glulam beams | EN 14080 | Strength class, durability |
| SIP panels | EN 14509 | Thermal, mechanical, fire |
| Insulation | EN 13162 (mineral wool) | Thermal conductivity, fire |
| Windows | EN 14351-1 | U-value, air permeability |
| Steel sections | EN 10025 | Strength grade |

## ModulCA Application
All structural and insulation products used in ModulCA modules must carry CE marking with valid Declarations of Performance. This ensures compliance when deploying modules across different EU markets.
