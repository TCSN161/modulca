# EU Fire Safety Framework

## Overview
The EU harmonises fire safety through the Euroclass system (reaction to fire) and fire resistance classification. National building codes set the actual requirements using these classes.

## Euroclass — Reaction to Fire (EN 13501-1)

| Class | Performance | Typical Materials |
|-------|------------|-------------------|
| A1 | Non-combustible, no contribution | Stone, concrete, steel, glass |
| A2 | Non-combustible, very limited contribution | Gypsum board, mineral wool |
| B | Very limited contribution to fire | Fire-retardant treated timber, some plastics |
| C | Limited contribution | Treated timber cladding |
| D | Acceptable contribution | Untreated timber, some composites |
| E | Acceptable, single flame test | Some insulation foams |
| F | No performance determined | Not classified |

Additional classifications:
- **s1/s2/s3** — Smoke production (s1 = least smoke)
- **d0/d1/d2** — Flaming droplets (d0 = no droplets)

## Fire Resistance (EN 13501-2)

| Symbol | Meaning | Test Criteria |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| R | Load-bearing capacity | Structural integrity under fire |
| E | Integrity | No flames/hot gases pass through |
| I | Insulation | Temperature on unexposed side |
| REI 30 | 30 minutes | Minimum for residential walls |
| REI 60 | 60 minutes | Standard for inter-storey floors |
| REI 90 | 90 minutes | Multi-storey or higher risk |
| REI 120 | 120 minutes | High-rise or special buildings |

## Residential Requirements (Typical)

| Element | Typical EU Requirement | Notes |
|---------|----------------------|-------|
| Load-bearing walls | REI 30–60 | Depends on height/occupancy |
| Floors between units | REI 60 | Fire compartmentation |
| External cladding | B-s2, d0 minimum | Post-Grenfell tightening |
| Roof covering | BROOF(t1) | External fire exposure |
| Escape routes | Non-combustible preferred | A2-s1, d0 or better |

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA modules achieve REI 60 through factory-installed Type F gypsum board (2×12.5mm) on structural elements. External cladding options are specified to meet B-s2,d0 minimum. Fire stops are pre-installed at all module-to-module penetrations.
