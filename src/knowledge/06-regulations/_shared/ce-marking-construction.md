# CE Marking for Construction Products

## Overview
CE marking is mandatory for construction products covered by harmonised European standards (hEN). It demonstrates conformity with the declared performance and enables free movement of products across the EU single market.

## How CE Marking Works

1. **Manufacturer** tests product according to the relevant hEN
2. **Declaration of Performance (DoP)** issued with essential characteristics
3. **CE mark** applied to product with reference to DoP
4. **Notified Body** involvement for system 1+, 1, or 2+ products

## Assessment Systems (AVCP)

| System | Manufacturer | Notified Body | Typical Products |
|--------|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| 1+ | Factory production control | Certification + audit + testing | Fire-rated products |
| 1 | Factory production control | Certification + audit | Structural steel, precast |
| 2+ | Testing + factory control | Audit of factory control | Insulation, windows, cladding |
| 3 | Factory production control | Initial type testing | Timber products |
| 4 | Testing + factory control | None | Non-structural products |

## Key Standards for Modular Construction

| Product | hEN | Essential Characteristics |
|---------|-----|--------------------------|
| Glulam beams | EN 14080 | Bending strength, stiffness |
| CLT panels | EN 16351 | Strength, fire, thermal |
| SIP panels | EN 14509 | Thermal, mechanical, fire |
| Mineral wool | EN 13162 | Thermal conductivity, fire |
| Windows | EN 14351-1 | U-value, air permeability |
| External doors | EN 14351-2 | U-value, security, fire |
| Steel sections | EN 10025 | Yield strength, impact |
| Concrete | EN 206 | Compressive strength |

## ModulCA Application
All structural and insulation products in ModulCA modules must carry CE marking. This is a procurement requirement — suppliers must provide valid DoPs. CE marking ensures that modules built for the Romanian market can use the same products when expanding to Netherlands, Germany, or other EU markets.
