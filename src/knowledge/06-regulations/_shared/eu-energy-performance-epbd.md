# EU Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD)

## Overview
The EPBD is the EU's main legislative instrument for improving the energy performance of buildings. All member states must transpose it into national law.

## Key Requirements

| Requirement | Target | Since |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| Nearly Zero Energy Buildings (nZEB) | All new buildings | 2021 |
| Energy Performance Certificates | Mandatory for sale/rent | 2002+ |
| Minimum energy performance standards | Renovation triggers | Varies |
| Zero-emission buildings | All new buildings | 2030 (recast) |
| Solar readiness | New buildings | 2027+ (recast) |

## National Transposition
Each country implements EPBD differently:
- **Romania**: C107/2005, nZEB since 2021
- **Netherlands**: BENG standards since 2021
- **Germany**: GEG (Gebäudeenergiegesetz) since 2020
- **France**: RE2020 since 2022

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA modules are designed to meet or exceed nZEB requirements across all target markets. The factory-controlled construction ensures consistent energy performance, and the flat roof design accommodates solar PV installation.
