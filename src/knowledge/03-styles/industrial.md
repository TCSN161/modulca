# Industrial Style

## Overview
Industrial style celebrates the raw beauty of construction — exposed structure, visible services, unfinished surfaces, and honest materials. Originating from converted warehouses and factories (loft living), it has become one of the most popular contemporary residential aesthetics, especially among young urban professionals.

## Key Characteristics

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Exposed structure | Visible beams, columns, connections |
| Raw materials | Concrete, steel, brick, timber |
| Open plan | Large undivided spaces |
| High ceilings | Double height or exposed roof structure |
| Visible services | Exposed ductwork, conduit, pipes |
| Metal accents | Steel, iron, copper, brass |
| Large windows | Factory-style glazing, steel frames |
| Minimal partitions | Glass or no dividers between zones |

## Material Palette

| Material | Application | Finish |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Concrete | Floors, accent walls | Polished or raw with formwork marks |
| Steel | Columns, beams, railings, furniture | Black matte, raw, or Corten |
| Brick | Accent walls | Exposed, whitewashed, or painted |
| Timber | Floors, ceiling, shelving | Rough-sawn, reclaimed, dark-stained |
| Glass | Partitions, shower screens | Clear, steel-framed |
| Copper/brass | Lighting, taps, hardware | Aged/patinated preferred |

## Industrial Rooms

| Room | Industrial Approach | Key Elements |
|------|-------------------|--------------|
| Kitchen | Open shelving, concrete counter, steel fixtures | Island as workshop bench |
| Living room | Exposed ceiling, concrete floor, oversized sofa | Factory pendant lights |
| Bedroom | Metal bed frame, exposed brick, minimal | Warm textiles to soften |
| Bathroom | Concrete basin, exposed copper pipes | Walk-in shower, black fixtures |
| Home office | Steel desk, factory lighting, pegboard | Utilitarian functional |
