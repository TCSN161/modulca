# Vitruvius — De Architectura (Ten Books on Architecture)

## Overview

Written around **30 BC** by Roman architect **Marcus Vitruvius Pollio**, *De Architectura* is the oldest surviving treatise on architecture. Its three principles — **Firmitas, Utilitas, Venustas** (Strength, Utility, Beauty) — still define what good architecture means 2,000 years later.

📚 **Free to read**: [Project Gutenberg →](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/20239) | [Archive.org →](https://archive.org/details/viaborArchitec)

## The Ten Books (Summary)

1. **Architecture and the Architect** — Training, knowledge required
2. **Building Materials** — Properties of stone, brick, timber, lime
3. **Temples (Ionic)** — Proportional systems, column orders
4. **Temples (Corinthian, Doric)** — More proportional rules
5. **Public Buildings** — Forums, basilicas, theatres
6. **Domestic Architecture** — Private houses, room orientation (★ most relevant)
7. **Interior Decoration** — Stucco, painting, color
8. **Water Supply** — Aqueducts, wells, lead pipes
9. **Astronomy & Sundials** — Geometry, timekeeping
10. **Machines** — Construction equipment, siege engines

## Why It Still Matters

Vitruvius established that architecture is not just building — it's the deliberate balance of **engineering + function + aesthetics**. Every subsequent architectural movement, from Renaissance to Modernism, has either built upon or reacted against these principles.

For ModulCA: our design philosophy directly maps to Vitruvius. A modular home that is structurally sound, functionally excellent, and beautifully designed fulfills the same 2,000-year-old standard.

## Book 6: Domestic Architecture (Key Insights)

- **Room orientation matters**: Living rooms face south/southwest (warmth + light)
- **Bedrooms face east**: Morning sun for natural wake cycles
- **Proportional rooms**: Length-to-width ratios of 3:5 or 2:3 feel balanced
- **Natural ventilation**: Cross-ventilation through opposing openings
- **Privacy gradient**: Public rooms at front, private at rear

These principles are embedded in ModulCA's AI-assisted layout recommendations.
