# Neufert — Architects' Data

## Overview

First published in 1936 by **Ernst Neufert**, *Architects' Data* (German: *Bauentwurfslehre*) is the single most important reference book in architecture. Now in its **43rd edition** (2019), updated by his son Peter Neufert, it remains the global standard for space planning, dimensions, and building design.

Every architecture student owns a copy. Every practicing architect references it. For ModulCA's modular construction approach, Neufert is the foundation of all room sizing.

## Why It Matters for ModulCA

ModulCA's 3×3m (9m²) module system is directly informed by Neufert standards:

| Room Type | Neufert Minimum | ModulCA Modules | Actual Size |
|-----------|----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Single bedroom | 8–10 m² | 1 module | 7 m² usable |
| Double bedroom | 12–16 m² | 2 modules | 14 m² usable |
| Living room | 20–30 m² | 3–4 modules | 21–28 m² usable |
| Kitchen | 8–12 m² | 1–2 modules | 7–14 m² usable |
| Bathroom | 4–6 m² | 1 module | 7 m² usable |

## Key Chapters for Residential Design

1. **Human Dimensions** — Reach zones, clearances, wheelchair accessibility
2. **Residential Buildings** — Room sizes, layouts, furniture zones
3. **Stairs & Ramps** — Rise/run ratios, headroom, handrail heights
4. **Kitchens** — Work triangle, counter heights, appliance zones
5. **Bathrooms** — Fixture clearances, wet zones, accessibility
6. **Doors & Windows** — Standard sizes, swing directions, daylighting
7. **Thermal Insulation** — U-values, condensation prevention
8. **Sound Insulation** — Decibel requirements, party wall construction

## Quick Reference: Essential Dimensions

### Clearances (from Neufert)
- **Corridor minimum width**: 900mm (1200mm recommended)
- **Door clear opening**: 800mm minimum (900mm accessible)
- **Staircase width**: 800mm minimum (1000mm recommended)
- **Kitchen work triangle**: 3.6–6.6m total (sum of 3 sides)
- **Bathroom fixture spacing**: 200mm between elements minimum

### Ceiling Heights
- **Residential minimum**: 2400mm (varies by country)
- **Recommended**: 2500–2700mm
- **ModulCA standard**: 2700mm (generous, modern feel)
