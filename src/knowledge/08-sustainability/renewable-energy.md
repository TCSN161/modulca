# Renewable Energy for Homes

## Overview
EU regulations (EPBD recast) increasingly require renewable energy in new buildings. Both Romania and the Netherlands have programs supporting residential renewable energy. For modular homes, the key systems are solar PV, heat pumps, and solar thermal.

## Solar Photovoltaic (PV)

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Panel power | 400-450Wp each | Current standard panels |
| Panel size | ~1.7 × 1.0m | ~1.7m² per panel |
| Roof area needed | ~6m² per kWp | Including spacing |
| Annual yield (Romania) | 1,100-1,350 kWh/kWp | South-facing, 30-35° tilt |
| Annual yield (Netherlands) | 850-1,000 kWh/kWp | South-facing, 35° tilt |
| System cost | €1,000-1,500/kWp installed | Declining annually |
| Payback period | 5-8 years (RO), 6-9 years (NL) | With net metering/subsidies |
| Lifespan | 25-30 years | Performance warranty |

## Heat Pumps

| Type | COP | Best For | Cost |
|------|-----|----------|------|
| Air-source (air-to-water) | 3.0-4.5 | Most homes, moderate climate | €5,000-10,000 |
| Air-source (air-to-air) | 3.5-5.0 | Supplement, per-room | €1,500-3,000 |
| Ground-source | 4.0-5.5 | Cold climate, large plots | €15,000-25,000 |
| Water-source | 4.0-5.5 | Near water body | €12,000-20,000 |

### Heat Pump Sizing
| Home Size | Heating Load (well-insulated) | Heat Pump Size |
|-----------|-------------------------------|----------------|
| Small (50-70m²) | 2.5-3.5 kW | 4-5 kW unit |
| Medium (80-120m²) | 4-6 kW | 6-8 kW unit |
| Large (130-180m²) | 6-9 kW | 8-12 kW unit |

## Battery Storage

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Useful capacity | 5-15 kWh per unit | Household scale |
| Round-trip efficiency | 90-95% | Lithium-ion |
| System cost | €500-800/kWh | Declining rapidly |
| Lifespan | 10-15 years | ~6,000 cycles |
| Self-consumption increase | +30-50% | Without battery: ~30%, with: 60-80% |

## Country-Specific Programs

### Romania
| Program | Benefit | Status |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Casa Verde PV | Up to 20,000 RON (~€4,000) for PV | Active |
| Casa Verde Heat Pump | Up to 16,000 RON (~€3,200) | Active |
| Net metering (prosumer) | Sell excess to grid | Active, regulated |
| Green certificates | Additional income from production | For larger systems |

### Netherlands
| Program | Benefit | Status |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Salderingsregeling | Net metering (being phased out) | Until ~2027 |
| ISDE subsidy | €500-2,500 for heat pump | Active |
| ISDE subsidy | €500-1,500 for solar boiler | Active |
| Aardgasvrij policy | No gas connections for new builds | Since 2018 |

## ModulCA Application
- **Flat roof = PV-ready**: Every module's flat roof can hold 4-5 panels (~1.8 kWp)
- **5-module home**: ~9 kWp system possible on roof — covers most household needs
- **Pre-wired**: Factory installs conduit and inverter mounting — PV panels mounted on-site
- **Heat pump module**: Outdoor unit on north side, indoor unit in utility module
- **Battery cabinet**: Dedicated space in utility module for home battery
- **No gas**: All-electric design aligns with Dutch aardgasvrij and Romanian nZEB trends
