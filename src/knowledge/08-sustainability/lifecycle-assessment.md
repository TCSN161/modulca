# Life Cycle Assessment of Modular Buildings

## Overview

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) evaluates the environmental impact of a building from raw material extraction through end-of-life. Modular construction shows significant advantages in several LCA categories, particularly in waste reduction, material efficiency, and end-of-life circularity.

## LCA Stages (EN 15978)

| Stage | Code | Description | Modular Impact |
|-------|------|-------------|---------------|
| Product | A1-A3 | Raw material, transport, manufacturing | Similar to traditional |
| Construction | A4-A5 | Transport to site, assembly | 30-50% lower |
| Use | B1-B7 | Maintenance, repair, energy use | 10-20% lower energy |
| End of life | C1-C4 | Demolition, transport, disposal | 60-80% lower waste |
| Beyond life | D | Reuse, recycling, recovery | Major advantage |

## Carbon Footprint Comparison

### Embodied Carbon (A1-A5)

Per m2 of floor area for a single-story residential building:

| Material System | kgCO2e/m2 | Notes |
|----------------|-----------|-------|
| Concrete block + brick | 450-550 | Cement is carbon-intensive |
| Light steel frame (modular) | 320-400 | Recyclable steel, less concrete |
| Timber frame (modular) | 180-250 | Carbon sequestration in wood |
| CLT modular | 150-220 | Highest sequestration |

### Operational Carbon (B6)

Annual CO2 emissions per m2:

| Building Standard | kgCO2e/m2/year | 30-Year Total |
|------------------|---------------|---------------|
| Romanian average existing | 35-45 | 1,050-1,350 |
| nZEB standard (25cm wall) | 12-18 | 360-540 |
| Enhanced (30cm wall) | 6-10 | 180-300 |
| Passive House | 3-5 | 90-150 |

### Total Life Cycle Carbon (30 years)

| Construction Type | Embodied | Operational (30yr) | Total kgCO2e/m2 |
|------------------|----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Traditional masonry | 500 | 1,200 | 1,700 |
| Modular steel 25cm | 360 | 450 | 810 |
| Modular steel 30cm | 400 | 240 | 640 |

**Modular construction reduces total lifecycle carbon by 50-62%.**

## Material Circularity

### Steel Frame Advantages

- **98% recyclable** at end of life (steel is infinitely recyclable)
- **Bolted connections** enable disassembly without damage
- **Scrap value**: Steel has positive end-of-life value (~€200/tonne)
- **Current recycled content**: 25-60% of structural steel is from recycled sources

### Disassembly and Reuse

Modular buildings are designed for deconstruction:

| Component | Reuse Potential | Recycling Rate |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| Steel frame | 85-95% reuse | 98% recycle |
| Mineral wool insulation | 40-60% reuse | 90% recycle |
| Gypsum boards | 20-30% reuse | 80% recycle |
| Windows/doors | 60-70% reuse | 85% recycle |
| Cladding panels | 50-70% reuse | 90% recycle |
| MEP systems | 30-40% reuse | 75% recycle |

### Construction Waste

| Metric | Traditional | Modular |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Waste generated | 80-120 kg/m2 | 15-25 kg/m2 |
| Landfill rate | 40-60% | 5-10% |
| Material waste rate | 10-15% | 2-4% |
| Water use on site | 1.5-2.5 m3/m2 | 0.1-0.3 m3/m2 |

## Environmental Product Declarations (EPD)

Modular manufacturers can obtain EPDs per EN 15804:
- **Type III EPD**: Third-party verified, internationally recognized
- **Scope**: Full module including all materials and factory energy
- **Validity**: 5 years
- **Cost**: €3,000-8,000 per product family
- **Advantage**: Enables green building certification (BREEAM, LEED, DGNB)

## Green Building Certification

Modular buildings can achieve:

| Certification | Achievable Level | Key Credits from Modular |
|--------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| BREEAM | Excellent-Outstanding | Materials, Waste, Energy |
| LEED | Gold-Platinum | MR credits, EA credits |
| DGNB | Gold-Platinum | ENV, ECO categories |
| Passive House | Classic-Premium | Air-tightness, U-values |

## Romanian Context

### EU Taxonomy Alignment

From 2027, Romanian construction projects seeking EU financing must demonstrate:
- **Climate mitigation**: Building lifecycle GWP below threshold
- **Circular economy**: 70% construction waste diverted from landfill
- **Pollution prevention**: Low-emission materials (no SVHC substances)

Modular construction naturally aligns with all three requirements.

### National Programs

- **Casa Verde** (AFM): Subsidies for renewable energy in residential buildings
- **Programul National de Redresare**: EU-funded building renovation and new construction
- **Green mortgage discounts**: Some banks offer 0.1-0.3% rate reduction for energy-efficient homes

## ModulCA Application

ModulCA provides estimated carbon footprint data per module configuration, helping users understand the environmental impact of their design choices. The Enhanced 30cm wall option is flagged as "Low Carbon" when it achieves below 700 kgCO2e/m2 total lifecycle.
