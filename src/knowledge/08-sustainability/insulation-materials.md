# Insulation Materials Comparison

## Overview
Choosing the right insulation material affects thermal performance, moisture behavior, fire safety, sustainability, and cost. For modular timber construction, the insulation must be compatible with wood framing and factory installation.

## Material Comparison

| Material | λ (W/mK) | Fire Class | Moisture | Sustainability | Cost (€/m² at R=5) |
|----------|----------|------------|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Mineral wool (glass) | 0.032-0.040 | A1 (non-combustible) | Breathable | Moderate | €15-25 |
| Mineral wool (stone/rock) | 0.035-0.042 | A1 | Breathable | Moderate | €18-30 |
| EPS (expanded polystyrene) | 0.030-0.038 | E (combustible) | Vapor barrier needed | Low (petroleum) | €10-18 |
| XPS (extruded polystyrene) | 0.028-0.036 | E | Closed-cell, waterproof | Low | €20-35 |
| PUR/PIR (polyurethane) | 0.022-0.028 | B-C | Closed-cell | Low | €25-40 |
| Wood fiber | 0.038-0.050 | E-D | Excellent hygroscopic | Very high | €25-45 |
| Sheep wool | 0.035-0.040 | B-C | Excellent hygroscopic | Very high | €30-50 |
| Cellulose (blown) | 0.035-0.042 | B-C (treated) | Good hygroscopic | Very high (recycled) | €12-20 |
| Hemp | 0.038-0.043 | E | Good hygroscopic | Very high | €25-40 |
| Cork | 0.037-0.050 | E | Good | Very high (renewable) | €35-60 |

## Best Fit for Modular Timber Construction

| Application | Recommended | Why |
|-------------|------------|-----|
| Wall cavity (between studs) | Mineral wool or cellulose | Easy to install in factory, fire-safe |
| External insulation (ETICS) | Wood fiber board | Breathable, compatible with timber |
| Roof | Mineral wool or PUR | Performance + fire safety |
| Floor (above screw piles) | XPS or PUR | Moisture-resistant, compressive strength |
| Acoustic separation | Mineral wool (dense) | Best sound absorption per thickness |
| Passive house upgrade | PUR/PIR + mineral wool combo | Thinnest for best U-value |

## Thickness Required for U-value Targets

| Target U-value | Mineral Wool (λ=0.035) | PUR (λ=0.025) | Wood Fiber (λ=0.042) |
|---------------|----------------------|----------------|---------------------|
| 0.28 W/m²K (C107 min) | 110mm | 80mm | 135mm |
| 0.22 W/m²K (nZEB) | 145mm | 100mm | 175mm |
| 0.15 W/m²K (Passive House) | 220mm | 155mm | 265mm |
| 0.10 W/m²K (Passive House+) | 330mm | 235mm | 400mm |

## ModulCA Application
- **Standard wall**: 120mm mineral wool between studs + 40mm wood fiber external = U ≈ 0.18 W/m²K
- **Factory installation**: Insulation fitted in controlled factory conditions — no gaps, no moisture
- **Transport protection**: Insulation pre-installed means modules are weather-resistant during transport
- **Upgrade path**: Swap mineral wool for PUR to achieve passive house in same wall thickness
- **Natural option**: Sheep wool or cellulose for clients wanting fully natural materials (biophilic tier)
