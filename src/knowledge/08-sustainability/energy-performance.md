# Energy Performance (nZEB)

## Overview
Nearly Zero Energy Building requirements applicable from 2021 in Romania / EU.

## Standards

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Primary energy demand | ≤ 100 kWh/m²yr | Romanian nZEB |
| Heating demand | ≤ 15 kWh/m²yr | Passive House level |
| Cooling demand | ≤ 15 kWh/m²yr | Passive House level |
| Airtightness target | ≤ 0.6 ACH @50Pa | Blower door test |
| Renewable energy share | ≥ 30% | Romanian EPBD transposition |
| PV panel area/module | ~8 m² | Flat roof, 3kW per module |
| Heat pump COP | ≥ 3.5 | Air-source, heating mode |

## ModulCA Application
Modular construction inherently supports nZEB through factory-controlled airtightness, consistent insulation, and pre-integrated renewable energy systems. Each ModulCA module can accommodate ~8m² of PV panels on its flat roof.
