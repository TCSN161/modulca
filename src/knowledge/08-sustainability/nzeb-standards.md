# Nearly Zero-Energy Building (nZEB) Standards

## Overview
Since January 1, 2021, all new buildings in the EU must be "nearly zero-energy buildings" (nZEB). Each member state defines its own nZEB thresholds based on the EU Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD). Both Romania and the Netherlands have national nZEB definitions.

## EU Framework

| Requirement | EPBD Definition |
|-------------|----------------|
| Very high energy performance | National minimum requirements |
| Nearly zero energy | Very low energy demand, covered mainly by renewables |
| Renewable energy | Produced on-site or nearby |
| Primary energy indicator | kWh/m² per year (primary energy) |

## Romania nZEB Requirements

| Parameter | Residential Value | Notes |
|-----------|------------------|-------|
| Primary energy demand | ≤ 100 kWh/m²·year | Includes heating, cooling, DHW, lighting |
| Heating demand | No explicit limit | Controlled by U-value requirements |
| Renewable energy share | ≥ 30% | Of total primary energy |
| U-value walls | ≤ 0.22 W/m²K | C107 updated for nZEB |
| U-value roof | ≤ 0.15 W/m²K | C107 updated |
| U-value floor | ≤ 0.25 W/m²K | C107 updated |
| U-value windows | ≤ 1.30 W/m²K | Triple glazing recommended |
| Airtightness | No mandatory test | Recommended ≤ 3.0 ACH @50Pa |
| Energy certificate | Mandatory | Class A minimum for new |

## Netherlands BENG Requirements

| Parameter | Residential Value | Notes |
|-----------|------------------|-------|
| BENG 1 (energy need) | ≤ 70 kWh/m²·year | Heating + cooling demand |
| BENG 2 (primary energy) | ≤ 50 kWh/m²·year | Primary fossil energy use |
| BENG 3 (renewable share) | ≥ 50% | Of total energy |
| U-value walls | ≤ 0.22 W/m²K (Rc ≥ 4.5) | BBL requirement |
| U-value roof | ≤ 0.17 W/m²K (Rc ≥ 6.0) | BBL requirement |
| U-value floor | ≤ 0.22 W/m²K (Rc ≥ 4.5) | BBL requirement |
| U-value windows | ≤ 1.65 W/m²K | But typically ≤ 1.0 for BENG |
| Airtightness | qv10 ≤ 0.4 dm³/s·m² | Mandatory calculation |
| TOjuli (overheating) | ≤ 1.20 | Summer comfort indicator |

## ModulCA Application
- **Factory precision**: Controlled manufacturing ensures consistent airtightness and insulation — easier to meet nZEB
- **Standard wall**: 0.18 W/m²K — exceeds both RO and NL requirements
- **PV-ready roof**: Flat roof supports solar panels — easy to meet renewable energy share
- **Heat pump standard**: Air-source heat pump as default heating — no gas needed
- **MVHR option**: Mechanical ventilation with heat recovery for BENG/Passive House performance
- **Dual compliance**: Same module design can meet both Romanian and Dutch nZEB — just adjust PV and ventilation
