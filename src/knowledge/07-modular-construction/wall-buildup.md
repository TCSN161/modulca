# Wall Assembly Layers

## Overview
Standard wall buildup for ModulCA modules — from exterior cladding to interior finish.

## Standards

| Layer | Thickness | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Total wall thickness | 265–300mm | Exterior to interior |
| Exterior cladding | 20mm | Timber, fibre-cement, or metal |
| Ventilated air gap | 25mm | Moisture drainage |
| Insulation (mineral wool) | 120mm | λ = 0.035 W/mK |
| Structural frame | 80mm | Steel C-section / timber stud |
| Vapour barrier | PE film | Sd ≥ 100m |
| Interior finish | 12.5–15mm | Gypsum board / plywood |
| Wall U-value | 0.18–0.22 W/m²K | Passive house zone |

## ModulCA Application
The wall assembly is factory-built as a complete unit, ensuring consistent quality and airtightness. The ventilated air gap behind the cladding prevents moisture buildup, while the vapour barrier on the warm side prevents interstitial condensation.
