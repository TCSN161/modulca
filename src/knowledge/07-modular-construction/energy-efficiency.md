# Energy Efficiency in Modular Buildings

## Overview

Modular construction offers inherent energy efficiency advantages over traditional building. Factory-controlled assembly ensures consistent insulation installation, precise air-tightness, and minimal thermal bridging — the three pillars of energy-efficient building.

## EU Requirements: Nearly Zero Energy Buildings (nZEB)

Since 2021, all new buildings in the EU must meet nZEB standards (EPBD recast). For Romania, this means:

| Metric | nZEB Requirement | ModulCA Standard (25cm) | ModulCA Enhanced (30cm) |
|--------|-----------------|------------------------|------------------------|
| Wall U-value | ≤ 0.30 W/(m2K) | 0.22 W/(m2K) | 0.15 W/(m2K) |
| Roof U-value | ≤ 0.20 W/(m2K) | 0.18 W/(m2K) | 0.12 W/(m2K) |
| Floor U-value | ≤ 0.30 W/(m2K) | 0.25 W/(m2K) | 0.18 W/(m2K) |
| Window U-value | ≤ 1.30 W/(m2K) | 1.10 W/(m2K) | 0.80 W/(m2K) |
| Air-tightness (n50) | ≤ 3.0 h-1 | 1.5 h-1 | 0.6 h-1 |
| Primary energy | ≤ 100 kWh/(m2a) | 65 kWh/(m2a) | 35 kWh/(m2a) |

## Why Modular Beats Traditional on Energy

### 1. Factory Precision

- **Insulation gaps**: Factory installation achieves 98%+ coverage vs. 85-90% on-site
- **Air barrier continuity**: Vapour barriers installed on flat surfaces in controlled conditions
- **Quality control**: Every module inspected before leaving the factory

### 2. Thermal Bridge Reduction

Common thermal bridges eliminated in modular construction:

| Thermal Bridge | Traditional | Modular Solution |
|---------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Wall-floor junction | Ψ = 0.15 W/(mK) | Ψ = 0.05 W/(mK) — gasket detailing |
| Window perimeter | Ψ = 0.10 W/(mK) | Ψ = 0.03 W/(mK) — factory-installed frame |
| Corner junction | Ψ = 0.08 W/(mK) | Ψ = 0.02 W/(mK) — continuous insulation |
| Module-to-module joint | N/A | Ψ = 0.04 W/(mK) — EPDM + insulation overlap |

### 3. Air-Tightness

Blower door test results (typical):
- **Traditional masonry**: n50 = 3.0-6.0 h-1
- **Modular standard**: n50 = 1.0-2.0 h-1
- **Modular enhanced**: n50 = 0.3-0.6 h-1 (Passive House level)

## Wall Assembly Performance

### Standard Exterior Wall (25cm)

```
Exterior Cladding (20mm)
  ↓
Ventilated Air Gap (25mm)
  ↓
Wind Barrier — MgO Board (10mm)
  ↓
Mineral Wool Insulation (100mm, λ=0.035)
  ↓
Steel Frame C-Studs (75mm)
  ↓
Vapour Barrier PE Film (5mm)
  ↓
Interior Plasterboard (15mm)
```

**Result**: U = 0.22 W/(m2K) — exceeds nZEB requirement by 27%

### Enhanced Wall (30cm)

```
Exterior Cladding (20mm)
  ↓
Ventilated Air Gap (25mm)
  ↓
Exterior Sheathing MgO Board (15mm)
  ↓
Mineral Wool Insulation (120mm, λ=0.032)
  ↓
Steel Frame SIP (80mm)
  ↓
Vapour Barrier (5mm)
  ↓
Service Cavity with Battens (20mm)
  ↓
Interior Plasterboard (15mm)
```

**Result**: U = 0.15 W/(m2K) — approaches Passive House standard

## Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)

In Romania, all new buildings require an EPC (Certificat de Performanta Energetica):
- **Class A** (< 75 kWh/m2a): ModulCA Enhanced wall + heat pump
- **Class B** (75-150 kWh/m2a): ModulCA Standard wall
- **Certification**: Must be issued by authorized energy auditor
- **Cost**: €200-500 for residential buildings
- **Validity**: 10 years
