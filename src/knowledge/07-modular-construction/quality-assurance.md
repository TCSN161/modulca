# Quality Assurance in Modular Factory Production

## Overview

Factory-based modular construction enables systematic quality control impossible to achieve on traditional building sites. Every module passes through defined inspection stations, and defects are caught before delivery — not after occupancy.

## Factory vs. Site Quality

| Quality Metric | Traditional (Site) | Modular (Factory) |
|---------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Insulation coverage | 85-92% | 98-100% |
| Air-tightness consistency | Varies ±40% | Varies ±10% |
| Dimensional accuracy | ±10mm | ±2mm |
| Defect discovery rate | 15-25% post-occupancy | 2-5% post-occupancy |
| Weather exposure during build | Full | Zero |
| Rework rate | 8-12% | 1-3% |

## Inspection Stages

### Stage 1: Steel Frame Assembly

- Dimensional check (laser measurement)
- Weld inspection (visual + ultrasonic for structural welds)
- Steel grade verification (material certificates)
- Corrosion protection check (galvanizing thickness: min 45 μm)

### Stage 2: Insulation Installation

- Thermal imaging scan (identifies gaps in insulation)
- Mineral wool density verification (min 30 kg/m3)
- Vapour barrier continuity check (smoke test or blower door)
- Thickness measurement at 8 points per wall panel

### Stage 3: Services (MEP)

- Electrical continuity and insulation resistance testing
- Plumbing pressure test (10 bar, 30 minutes, zero drop)
- Ventilation duct air-tightness class verification
- Fire stopping at all service penetrations

### Stage 4: Interior Finishing

- Plasterboard fixing pattern (screw spacing ≤ 300mm)
- Surface flatness (≤ 3mm per 2m straightedge)
- Paint/finish consistency (visual inspection under controlled lighting)
- Floor level check (≤ 2mm per 2m)

### Stage 5: Pre-Delivery

- Overall dimensional verification
- Weight measurement (transport compliance)
- Lifting point structural test (1.5x design load)
- Documentation package completeness
- Photographic record of all concealed works

## Required Certifications

### For the Factory

| Certification | Standard | Scope |
|--------------|----------|-------|
| Quality Management | ISO 9001:2015 | Overall production system |
| Steel Fabrication | SR EN 1090-1 (EXC2 min) | Structural steel welding and assembly |
| Environmental | ISO 14001:2015 | Environmental management (optional but valued) |
| Occupational Safety | ISO 45001:2018 | Worker safety (required for EU tenders) |

### For the Building

| Certification | Authority | When Required |
|--------------|-----------|---------------|
| CE Marking | EU / SR EN 1090 | All structural steel products |
| Agrément Tehnic | MDLPA Romania | Novel construction systems |
| Energy Certificate | Authorized auditor | Before occupancy |
| ISC Conformity | ISC (State Inspectorate) | All buildings in Romania |

## Romanian Regulatory Framework

### ISC (Inspectoratul de Stat in Constructii)

ISC oversees construction quality in Romania:
- **Factory inspection**: ISC can inspect modular factories producing for Romanian market
- **On-site inspection**: Required at foundation and assembly stages
- **Reception process**: Technical reception (receptie la terminarea lucrarilor) required before occupancy
- **Guarantee periods**: 5 years general, 10 years structural

### Responsible Parties (HG 273/1994)

| Role | Responsibility | Qualification |
|------|---------------|---------------|
| Designer (proiectant) | Structural & architectural design | MLPAT attestation |
| Checker (verificator) | Independent design review | MLPAT attestation (A1, A2, etc.) |
| Builder (constructor) | Execution quality | ISC authorization |
| Site supervisor (diriginte de santier) | On-site quality control | MLPAT attestation |
| Technical expert | Assess existing structures | MLPAT attestation |

## Common Quality Issues and Prevention

| Issue | Prevention | Detection Method |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| Thermal bridging at joints | Factory-installed thermal breaks | Thermal imaging |
| Moisture in closed panels | Humidity monitoring during production | Moisture meter (< 18% for timber elements) |
| Transport damage | Protective wrapping + GPS shock monitoring | Pre/post-transport photo comparison |
| Foundation misalignment | Survey-grade leveling before module placement | Laser level (tolerance ±3mm) |
| Acoustic flanking | Resilient gaskets at all module joints | Post-assembly acoustic test |
