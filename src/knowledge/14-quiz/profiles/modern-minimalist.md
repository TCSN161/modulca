# Modern Minimalist Profile

## Style Summary

The Modern Minimalist home is defined by clean geometry, restrained material palettes, and a deliberate absence of clutter. Every element serves a purpose. Open-plan living areas flow seamlessly into one another, with floor-to-ceiling glazing connecting interior spaces to the landscape. The result is a home that feels larger than its footprint suggests.

## Recommended Materials

- **Structure:** CLT (cross-laminated timber) panels or steel frame with timber infill
- **Exterior cladding:** Fiber cement panels in white or light gray, smooth render
- **Windows:** Large-format aluminum-clad timber frames, triple-glazed, minimal mullions
- **Interior walls:** Smooth white plaster or microcement finish
- **Flooring:** Polished concrete, large-format porcelain tiles, or engineered oak in light tones
- **Roof:** Flat roof with EPDM membrane, optional extensive green roof

## Suggested Module Layout

A typical Modern Minimalist home uses **4-6 modules** (each 3x3m = 9m2):

| Module | Function | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| 1-2 | Open-plan living + kitchen | Combined space, kitchen island as divider |
| 3 | Master bedroom | Walk-in closet integrated into wall system |
| 4 | Bathroom + utility | Concealed laundry, wall-hung fixtures |
| 5 | Entry + storage | Mudroom with hidden built-ins |
| 6 (optional) | Home office or guest room | Dual-purpose with fold-away bed |

## Room Priorities

1. Living/kitchen open space (largest allocation)
2. Master bedroom with integrated storage
3. Bathroom - spa-like, minimal fixtures
4. Entry sequence with concealed storage
5. Flexible secondary room

## Sustainability Level

**Moderate to High.** The minimalist approach inherently reduces material waste. Flat roofs support solar panel installation. Large glazing requires high-performance glass (U-value below 0.8) to balance thermal performance with transparency. Recommended insulation: 200mm+ mineral wool or wood fiber.

## Budget Indicator

**Mid-High range: 1400-1800 EUR/m2.** Premium glazing and concealed storage systems increase cost above standard mid-range. Savings come from restrained material palette and efficient module count. Total for a 4-module home (36m2 usable): approximately 50,000-65,000 EUR structure and finishes.
