# Section 5: Practical Constraints

Real-world factors like budget, timeline, and site conditions shape what is achievable. This section ensures your profile recommendations stay grounded and actionable.

## Questions

### Q1: What is your approximate budget per square meter of built area?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) 800-1200 EUR/m2 - economy range, standard finishes
  - B) 1200-1600 EUR/m2 - mid range, good quality materials
  - C) 1600-2000 EUR/m2 - premium range, high-end finishes
  - D) 2000+ EUR/m2 - luxury range, bespoke materials and systems
- **Scoring:** budget_tier: A=economy, B=mid, C=premium, D=luxury
- **Affects:** material grade, system complexity, finish level, profile feasibility filtering

### Q2: How urgent is your timeline?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) As soon as possible - I need to move in within 3-4 months
  - B) 6 months - comfortable but goal-oriented
  - C) 1 year - no rush, willing to plan carefully
  - D) Flexible - quality matters more than speed
- **Scoring:** timeline_pressure: A=5, B=3, C=2, D=1
- **Affects:** prefabrication level, parallel vs sequential phasing, permit timeline buffer, finish selection lead times

### Q3: What is your land situation?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) I own a plot and have building permits or can obtain them
  - B) I am actively looking for land
  - C) Rural location - larger plot, fewer restrictions
  - D) Urban location - smaller plot, stricter regulations
  - E) Suburban - moderate plot size, standard zoning
- **Scoring:** land_context: A=ready, B=searching, C=rural, D=urban, E=suburban
- **Affects:** module footprint constraints, height limits, setback requirements, foundation type, site access for delivery

### Q4: How many floors do you want?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Single story (parter) - all living on one level
  - B) 1.5 stories (parter + mansarda) - bedrooms in the attic level
  - C) Two full stories (parter + etaj) - distinct ground and upper floor
- **Scoring:** floors: A=1, B=1.5, C=2
- **Affects:** structural module requirements, staircase module inclusion, foundation sizing, roof form, accessibility

### Q5: Do you have specific accessibility requirements?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) None currently needed
  - B) Future-proofing - I want the home adaptable for aging in place
  - C) Current need - wheelchair accessibility or mobility considerations required now
- **Scoring:** accessibility_level: A=standard, B=adaptable, C=full_accessible
- **Affects:** corridor width (standard 90cm vs accessible 120cm), door width, bathroom layout, ramp or lift module, single-floor priority, threshold design
