# Section 2: Space Priorities

How you prioritize different rooms and spaces directly shapes your modular floor plan. This section maps your preferences to specific module allocations.

## Questions

### Q1: Rank the following rooms by importance (1 = most important)
- **Type:** ranking
- **Options:**
  - Kitchen
  - Living room
  - Master bedroom
  - Bathroom(s)
  - Home office
  - Storage/utility
- **Scoring:** top_3_rooms receive priority module placement and larger allocation
- **Affects:** module arrangement hierarchy, adjacency planning

### Q2: Do you prefer generous storage or open, spacious rooms?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Maximum storage - built-in closets, pantry, utility room
  - B) Balanced - adequate storage without sacrificing openness
  - C) Open space priority - minimal visible storage, clean surfaces
  - D) Hidden storage - creative solutions that maintain open feel
- **Scoring:** storage_weight: A=5, B=3, C=1, D=4
- **Affects:** utility module inclusion, wall-integrated storage design, closet module count

### Q3: What does your morning routine look like?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Quick - 15 minutes, shower and go
  - B) Standard - 30 minutes, comfortable pace
  - C) Relaxed - 45+ minutes, enjoy the process
  - D) Shared - multiple people need the bathroom simultaneously
- **Scoring:** bathroom_priority: A=1, B=2, C=4, D=5
- **Affects:** bathroom count, bathroom module size, dual-sink consideration, ensuite priority

### Q4: Do you need a dedicated home office or workspace?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) No - a corner of the living room is fine
  - B) A nook or alcove - semi-separated space
  - C) A dedicated room - with door for privacy and video calls
  - D) A studio - large workspace for creative or technical work
- **Scoring:** office_modules: A=0, B=0.5, C=1, D=2
- **Affects:** office module allocation, acoustic isolation level, electrical and data infrastructure

### Q5: How often do you have overnight guests?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Rarely or never
  - B) A few times per year
  - C) Monthly
  - D) Frequently - family visits, hosting culture
- **Scoring:** guest_priority: A=0, B=1, C=3, D=4
- **Affects:** guest bedroom module, convertible space design, second bathroom justification

### Q6: Do you need space for hobbies, workshops, or special activities?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) No special space needed
  - B) Small area - crafts, music practice, exercise mat
  - C) Dedicated room - home gym, art studio, music room
  - D) Workshop - woodworking, mechanical, large equipment
- **Scoring:** hobby_modules: A=0, B=0.5, C=1, D=2
- **Affects:** additional module allocation, floor reinforcement, ventilation, noise isolation
