# Section 1: Lifestyle Analysis

Understanding your daily routines and household composition helps ModulCA recommend the right number of modules and room priorities for your home.

## Questions

### Q1: How many people will live in this home?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) 1 person (solo living)
  - B) 2 people (couple)
  - C) 3-4 people (small family)
  - D) 5+ people (large family)
- **Scoring:** modules_min: A=2, B=3, C=5, D=8
- **Affects:** total module count, bedroom count, bathroom count

### Q2: How often do you work from home?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Never - I always work outside the home
  - B) Occasionally - 1-2 days per week
  - C) Frequently - 3-4 days per week
  - D) Full-time remote - 5+ days per week
- **Scoring:** office_priority: A=0, B=1, C=3, D=5
- **Affects:** dedicated office module, acoustic separation, network infrastructure

### Q3: How would you describe your cooking habits?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Minimal - mostly quick meals and takeout
  - B) Regular - daily home cooking for the household
  - C) Enthusiast - elaborate meals, multiple courses, baking
  - D) Professional-level - large-scale cooking, entertaining guests with food
- **Scoring:** kitchen_priority: A=1, B=3, C=4, D=5
- **Affects:** kitchen module size, pantry allocation, ventilation requirements

### Q4: How do you prefer to entertain or spend leisure time at home?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Quiet activities - reading, music, small conversations
  - B) Media focused - movies, gaming, streaming
  - C) Social gatherings - regular dinners and parties with friends
  - D) Mixed - a combination of all the above
- **Scoring:** living_priority: A=2, B=3, C=5, D=4
- **Affects:** living room module sizing, open-plan vs separated layout, acoustic zones

### Q5: How much time do you spend outdoors at home?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) Minimal - I prefer being inside
  - B) Moderate - morning coffee or evening relaxation on a terrace
  - C) Significant - gardening, outdoor dining, reading outside
  - D) Extensive - outdoor living is central to my lifestyle
- **Scoring:** outdoor_priority: A=0, B=2, C=4, D=5
- **Affects:** terrace module inclusion, garden integration, indoor-outdoor flow design

### Q6: Do you have or plan to have pets?
- **Type:** single-choice
- **Options:**
  - A) No pets
  - B) Small pets (cats, small dogs, fish)
  - C) Large dogs or multiple pets
  - D) Hobby animals (chickens, rabbits, etc.)
- **Scoring:** pet_considerations: A=0, B=1, C=3, D=4
- **Affects:** flooring material choices, entry/mudroom module, outdoor enclosure needs, storage for supplies
