# Plumbing & Water

## Overview
Pipe sizing, fixture flows, and drainage for kitchen and bathroom modules.

## Standards

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Cold water supply | 15mm (½") PE | Main entry |
| Hot water supply | 15mm copper/PEX | From boiler/heat pump |
| Sink drain | 40mm | Kitchen and vanity |
| Shower drain | 50mm | Minimum flow rate |
| Toilet drain | 110mm | EN 12056 |
| Vent stack | 75mm | Through roof |
| Hot water temp | 55–60°C | Legionella prevention |
| Water pressure | 1.5–3.0 bar | At fixture |
| Floor drain slope | 1:80 min. | Towards drain |

## ModulCA Application
Plumbing is concentrated in "wet modules" (kitchen and bathroom) with all pipes pre-installed in the factory. Module-to-module connections use push-fit couplings (15–22mm) for quick on-site assembly.
