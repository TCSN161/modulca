# HVAC Sizing Guide

## Overview
Quick sizing rules for heating, cooling, and ventilation in modular homes.

## Standards

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Heating load (well-insulated) | 30–50 W/m² | SIP/CLT construction |
| Cooling load | 40–60 W/m² | Continental climate |
| Ventilation rate | 0.5 ACH | Or 30 m³/h per person |
| Kitchen extract | 60 l/s | During cooking |
| Bathroom extract | 15 l/s | Intermittent |
| Mini-split per module | 2.5–3.5 kW | For 7 m² interior |
| MVHR efficiency | ≥ 85% | Heat recovery ventilation |
| Duct diameter | 125–160mm | Main supply/extract |

## ModulCA Application
Each 3×3m module (~7m² usable) requires a 2.5–3.5 kW mini-split unit for heating/cooling. MVHR (Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery) at ≥85% efficiency is recommended for all ModulCA homes to maintain air quality while minimizing energy loss.
