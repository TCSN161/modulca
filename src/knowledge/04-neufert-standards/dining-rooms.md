# Dining Room & Eating Spaces

## Overview
Neufert standards for dining areas — standalone rooms, open-plan dining, and breakfast nooks.

## Table Sizes

| Seating | Rectangular | Round | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|-------|
| 2 people | 80 × 80cm | ∅ 80cm | Breakfast table |
| 4 people | 120 × 80cm | ∅ 100cm | Small family |
| 6 people | 160 × 90cm | ∅ 120cm | Standard family |
| 8 people | 200 × 100cm | ∅ 140cm | Entertaining |
| 10+ people | 240 × 100cm | ∅ 160cm | Large gatherings |

## Space Per Person

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Width per place setting | 600–700mm | Along table edge |
| Depth per setting | 400mm | Table surface use |
| Chair pullback space | 750mm | From table edge to wall |
| Serving circulation | 900mm | Behind seated diners |
| Buffet/sideboard depth | 400–500mm | Plus 900mm front clearance |

## Room Areas

| Configuration | Area | Notes |
|--------------|------|-------|
| Dining for 4 | 8–10m² | Separate room |
| Dining for 6 | 12–14m² | Comfortable |
| Kitchen-dining | 14–18m² | Combined function |
| Open living/dining | 25–35m² | Most popular modern layout |

## ModulCA Application
- **Kitchen-dining** (2 modules): One module kitchen + one module dining with table for 4-6
- **Open plan** (3-4 modules): Living + dining combined, table for 6-8
- Island counter with bar stools can serve as casual dining in a single kitchen module
