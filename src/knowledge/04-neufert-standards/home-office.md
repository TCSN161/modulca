# Home Office & Study Design

## Overview
Neufert standards for home workspace design — increasingly critical as remote work becomes standard.

## Space Requirements

| Configuration | Area | Notes |
|--------------|------|-------|
| Minimum office | 6m² | Desk + chair + shelf |
| Comfortable office | 8–10m² | Desk + meeting corner |
| Dual workspace | 12–15m² | Two desks or desk + table |
| Office corner (in room) | 3–4m² | Desk area within bedroom/living |

## Desk & Chair Ergonomics

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Desk height | 720–760mm | EN 527 standard |
| Desk depth | ≥ 800mm | For monitor + keyboard |
| Desk width | ≥ 1200mm | Single monitor workspace |
| Chair seat height | 420–510mm | Adjustable range |
| Monitor distance | 500–700mm | Arm's length |
| Monitor top edge | At eye level | Prevent neck strain |
| Leg clearance under desk | ≥ 600mm deep × 650mm wide | Free movement |
| Chair movement zone | 1000mm behind desk | Rolling space |

## Lighting & Environment

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Task lighting | 500 lux | On desk surface |
| Natural light | Preferred from side | Avoid glare on screen |
| Window position | 90° to screen | Not behind or facing |
| Noise level | ≤ 40 dB(A) | Concentration standard |
| Temperature | 20–22°C | Thermal comfort for sedentary work |
| Ventilation | 30 m³/h per person | Fresh air requirement |

## ModulCA Application
- **1 module** (7m² usable): Dedicated home office — exceeds 6m² minimum. Fits L-desk, bookshelf, and filing
- **Hybrid module**: Bedroom + office corner — use 2 modules with zoning
- Window on side wall for side-lighting (no screen glare)
- Pre-wire with 6+ socket outlets and ethernet/data cable
