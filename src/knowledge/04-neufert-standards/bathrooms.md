# Bathroom Design Standards

## Overview
Neufert standards for bathroom design including fixture clearances and ventilation.

## Standards

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Minimum area (WC+shower+basin) | 4m² | Basic bathroom |
| Full bathroom (with bathtub) | 6–8m² | Comfortable |
| Toilet side clearance | 0.20m each side | From wall |
| Toilet front clearance | 0.60m | From bowl to wall/door |
| Shower minimum | 0.80 × 0.80m | 0.90 × 0.90m recommended |
| Basin height | 0.80–0.85m | Standard mounting |
| Ventilation | min 40 l/s or window 1/10 of floor | Natural or mechanical |

## ModulCA Application
One 3×3m module (7m² usable) comfortably fits a full bathroom with shower, vanity, and toilet, exceeding the 4m² minimum. All plumbing is pre-installed in the factory as a complete wet module, ready for quick connection on site.
