# Laundry & Utility Room Standards

## Overview
Neufert standards for utility spaces — laundry, storage, mechanical rooms, and service areas.

## Laundry Room

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Minimum area | 4–5m² | Washer + dryer + folding |
| Comfortable area | 6–8m² | With ironing and sorting |
| Washer space | 600 × 600mm | Plus 50mm service gap |
| Dryer space | 600 × 600mm | Or stacked on washer |
| Folding surface | 800 × 600mm min | Countertop or table |
| Sink (optional) | 500 × 400mm | For hand washing |
| Drying rack space | 1.5m × 0.6m | When extended |

## Utility / Technical Room

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Heat pump / boiler room | 3–4m² | With service clearance |
| Electrical panel space | 600 × 200mm | Plus 800mm front clearance |
| Water meter space | 400 × 300mm | Accessible for reading |
| MVHR unit space | 600 × 600mm | Wall or ceiling mounted |
| Hot water tank | ∅ 500mm × 1500mm | 200L cylinder |

## Storage

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Min per dwelling | 2m² | Neufert minimum |
| Recommended | 4–6m² | Practical for family |
| Shelf depth | 400–600mm | Standard shelving |
| Walk-in closet | 4–6m² | Premium option |

## ModulCA Application
- **Half-module utility zone**: 3.5m² within a module can house washer, dryer, and heat pump
- **Dedicated utility module**: One 3×3m module (7m² usable) for laundry + technical + storage
- All plumbing and electrical pre-connected for wet appliances
