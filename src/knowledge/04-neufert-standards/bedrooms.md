# Bedroom Design Standards

## Overview
Detailed Neufert standards for bedroom design including bed sizes, wardrobe requirements, and optimal layouts.

## Bed Sizes (European Standard)

| Type | Width × Length | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Single | 90 × 200cm | Standard adult |
| Double | 140 × 200cm | Compact double |
| Queen | 160 × 200cm | Standard couple |
| King | 180 × 200cm | Generous couple |
| Super King | 200 × 200cm | Premium |
| Children's | 70 × 140cm | Toddler; 90 × 200cm school-age |

## Room Layout Standards

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Single bedroom min. | 10m² (Neufert) / 8m² (RO) | With bed + wardrobe + desk |
| Double bedroom min. | 14m² (Neufert) / 12m² (RO) | With bed + 2 wardrobes |
| Master suite | 16–20m² | Bedroom + en-suite access |
| Bed side clearance | ≥ 600mm | One accessible side minimum |
| Bed foot clearance | ≥ 800mm | To wall or furniture |
| Wardrobe depth | 600mm | Standard clothing depth |
| Wardrobe front clearance | ≥ 800mm | Door opening + standing |

## Ventilation & Light

| Parameter | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Window area | ≥ 1/8 of floor area | Natural light requirement |
| Openable window | ≥ 1/20 of floor area | Natural ventilation |
| Blackout provision | Recommended | For sleep quality |
| Noise level | ≤ 30 dB(A) | Nighttime standard |

## Storage Requirements

| Household Member | Wardrobe Length | Notes |
|-----------------|----------------|-------|
| Adult | 1.00–1.20m | Hanging + shelves |
| Child | 0.60–0.80m | Grows over time |
| Guest room | 0.60m | Minimal provision |

## ModulCA Application
- **1 module** (7m² usable): Fits single bedroom with 90cm bed, nightstand, and 1.2m wardrobe
- **2 modules** (14m² usable): Comfortable double bedroom with queen bed, two wardrobes, dresser
- **2 modules + 1** (21m² usable): Master suite with walk-in area and en-suite bathroom door
