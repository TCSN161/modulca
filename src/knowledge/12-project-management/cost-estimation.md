# Modular Construction Costs (Romania 2024–2025)

## Overview
Cost breakdown for modular construction in Romania.

## Cost Breakdown

| Component | Cost per m² | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Basic module | €800–1,200/m² | Structure + insulation + finishes |
| Premium module | €1,200–1,800/m² | High-end finishes, smart home |
| Foundation | €50–100/m² | Screw piles or strip |
| Transport | €500–2,000/module | Distance-dependent |
| Assembly | €100–200/m² | Crane + labor |
| **Total turnkey** | **€1,000–2,000/m²** | Depending on specification |

## Comparison
Traditional construction in Romania: €800–1,500/m². Modular construction is cost-competitive while being 40–60% faster in total build time.

## ModulCA Application
ModulCA's pricing model is based on the module count and specification level (Basic, Premium). Transport costs vary by distance from the factory. Assembly costs are predictable due to standardized connections.
