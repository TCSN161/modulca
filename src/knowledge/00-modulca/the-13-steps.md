# The 13 Steps — ModulCA Design Process

## Overview
ModulCA guides you through 13 steps to design your modular home. Each step builds on the previous one, taking you from choosing land to a complete presentation-ready project.

## The 13 Steps

### Step 1: Land Selection 🗺️
**What**: Choose or analyze your building plot
**You do**: Enter your land location, dimensions, and shape
**The system**: Shows the plot on a map, calculates buildable area, checks orientation
**Key info needed**: Land dimensions, location, any restrictions from Certificat de Urbanism (CU)

### Step 2: Marketplace 🏪
**What**: Browse available terrain options and pre-configured house packages
**You do**: Explore available lots and building packages
**The system**: Shows pricing, specifications, and availability
**Output**: Selected lot or confirmed custom project start

### Step 3: Design (Layout) 📐
**What**: Design your floor plan using 3×3m modules
**You do**: Drag and drop modules onto a grid, assign room functions
**The system**: Validates layout, calculates areas, checks minimum room sizes
**Key decisions**: Number of modules, room layout, open-plan vs. separate rooms
**Module types**: Living, bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, office, corridor, stairs, utility

### Step 4: Output (Preview) 👁️
**What**: Review your design in 2D with dimensions and areas
**You do**: Check room sizes, circulation, and overall layout
**The system**: Shows floor plan with dimensions, total area, room schedule
**Output**: Verified floor plan ready for styling

### Step 5: Style Selection 🎨
**What**: Choose the architectural style and visual direction
**You do**: Browse style options, select mood board elements
**The system**: Shows style-matched material palettes and facade options
**Styles**: Modern minimalist, Scandinavian, Mediterranean, Industrial, Traditional, Organic

### Step 6: Configure ⚙️
**What**: Configure detailed room specifications
**You do**: Choose finish levels, floor materials, wall finishes per room
**The system**: Updates cost estimates based on selections
**Finish levels**: Basic (€800-1,200/m²), Premium (€1,200-1,800/m²)

### Step 7: Visualize 🏠
**What**: Explore your design in interactive 3D
**You do**: Walk through the 3D model, check spatial feel
**The system**: Renders 3D model with furniture, materials, and lighting
**Features**: Rotate, zoom, enter rooms, see furniture placement

### Step 8: Render 🖼️
**What**: Generate photorealistic images of your home
**You do**: Choose camera angles, time of day, season
**The system**: AI generates photorealistic exterior and interior renders
**Providers**: Multiple AI image providers with automatic fallback
**Output**: High-quality images for visualization and presentation

### Step 9: Technical 📋
**What**: Review technical specifications and construction details
**You do**: Explore the knowledge base, check structural details
**The system**: Shows technical drawings, construction details, regulations
**Includes**: Floor plans, sections, elevations, wall details, MEP layouts, foundation plans

### Step 10: Walkthrough 🚶
**What**: Immersive walkthrough experience
**You do**: Walk through your home in first-person view
**The system**: 3D walkthrough with realistic proportions and materials
**Features**: First-person camera, room-to-room navigation

### Step 11: Products 🛒
**What**: Select products, fixtures, and finishes from the marketplace
**You do**: Browse and select kitchen appliances, bathroom fixtures, flooring, etc.
**The system**: Calculates product costs, shows installation details
**Output**: Product specification list with costs

### Step 12: Finalize ✅
**What**: Review and confirm your complete project
**You do**: Final review of all selections, costs, and specifications
**The system**: Generates project summary with total cost estimate
**Output**: Complete project specification ready for presentation

### Step 13: Presentation 📑
**What**: Generate professional PDF presentation
**You do**: Choose presentation format and content
**The system**: Creates PDF with floor plans, renders, specs, and costs
**Use for**: Building permit applications, contractor quotes, bank financing, personal records

## Tips for Best Results
1. **Start with your needs**: How many people? How many bedrooms? Work from home?
2. **Check your CU first**: The Certificat de Urbanism defines what you can build
3. **Think about orientation**: South-facing living areas get the most sunlight
4. **Don't over-build**: Modular means you can add modules later
5. **Ask me anything**: I know Neufert standards, Romanian/Dutch regulations, and modular construction
