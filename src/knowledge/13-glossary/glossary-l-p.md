# Glossary L-P

| Term | Definition | Related Article |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| LCA (Life Cycle Assessment) | Methodology to evaluate the environmental impact of a building or product across its full life cycle: raw material extraction, manufacturing, use, and end-of-life. Measured in kg CO2-eq. | lca-embodied-carbon |
| Live load | Variable loads on a structure from occupants, furniture, equipment, and snow. Residential floors typically designed for 1.5-2.0 kN/m2 per Eurocode 1. | eurocodes-overview |
| LOE (ES) | Ley de Ordenacion de la Edificacion -- Spain's Building Planning Act defining roles and responsibilities of all parties involved in the construction process. | |
| Loft | An upper-level space within a pitched roof, often used as a bedroom or storage area in modular homes. Minimum headroom of 2.1 m required over at least 50% of usable area (varies by country). | ceiling-heights |
| MEP | Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing -- the building services systems including HVAC, electrical wiring, water supply, drainage, and fire protection. Pre-installed in factory for modular units. | hvac-sizing |
| Mineral wool | Fibrous insulation made from molten stone (rock wool) or recycled glass (glass wool). Non-combustible (Euroclass A1/A2), good acoustic performance, lambda typically 0.032-0.040 W/mK. | insulation-materials |
| Module (ModulCA) | The base spatial unit in the ModulCA platform: a 3 m x 3 m grid cell. Homes are composed by combining modules; standard configurations range from 2x3 (36 m2) to 4x5 (180 m2). | module-system |
| Moisture barrier | A membrane or coating that prevents liquid water from penetrating the building envelope. Distinct from a vapor barrier, which controls water vapor diffusion. | wall-buildup |
| NEN (NL) | Nederlands Normalisatie-instituut -- Dutch standards body publishing NEN norms referenced by Bouwbesluit. Examples: NEN 1010 (electrical), NEN 7120 (energy performance, now replaced by BENG). | bouwbesluit-overview |
| NZEB (Nearly Zero-Energy Building) | EU-mandated standard requiring all new buildings to have very low energy demand, mostly covered by renewable sources. National definitions vary (BENG in NL, nZEB in RO). | nzeb-standards |
| NTC (IT) | Norme Tecniche per le Costruzioni -- Italy's structural design code. Incorporates Eurocodes with Italian national parameters, including seismic zonation. | eurocodes-overview |
| Omgevingswet (NL) | Dutch Environment and Planning Act (effective 2024). Merges 26 former laws into one framework governing spatial planning, environmental permits, and building regulations. | omgevingswet |
| OSB (Oriented Strand Board) | Engineered wood panel made from compressed wood strands bonded with resin. Used for wall sheathing, floor decking, and roof panels in timber-frame construction. OSB/3 grade required for structural humid conditions. | timber-frame |
| P100 (RO) | Romanian seismic design code (P100-1/2013). Defines seismic zones, design ground acceleration (ag), and structural requirements. All buildings in Romania must comply. | seismic-p100 |
| Passive House (Passivhaus) | Ultra-low energy building standard originating in Germany. Requires heating demand below 15 kWh/m2/yr, airtightness n50 below 0.6 ACH, and total primary energy under 120 kWh/m2/yr. | passive-house |
| PEB (BE) | Performance Energetique des Batiments -- Belgian (Wallonia/Brussels) energy performance framework with requirements for insulation, airtightness, and renewable energy. | energy-performance |
| Permesso di costruire (IT) | Italian building permit issued by the municipality (Comune). Required for new construction and major renovations; subject to urban planning (PRG) compliance. | |
| PLU (FR) | Plan Local d'Urbanisme -- French local zoning plan defining permitted land uses, building heights, setbacks, and density. Must be consulted before any building project. | |
| PV (Photovoltaic) | Solar panels that convert sunlight directly into electricity. Roof-mounted PV is a primary strategy for achieving NZEB/BENG targets in modular homes. | renewable-energy |
