# Glossary A-D

| Term | Definition | Related Article |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| Accessibility | Design principles ensuring buildings are usable by people of all abilities, including wheelchair users. Governed by national norms (e.g., NP 051 in Romania, Bouwbesluit Ch. 4 in NL). | accessibility |
| Airtightness | The degree to which a building envelope prevents uncontrolled air leakage. Measured in air changes per hour at 50 Pa (n50). Critical for energy performance and Passive House certification. | passive-house |
| Aparejador (ES) | Spanish term for a technical architect or quantity surveyor who oversees construction execution and compliance on site. | |
| Autorizatie de construire (RO) | Romanian building permit (Autorizatie de Construire / AC). Required before any construction work begins; issued by the local municipality based on an approved project. | building-permit-process |
| BENG (NL) | Bijna Energieneutrale Gebouwen -- Dutch near-zero energy building standard. Defines three indicators: energy demand (kWh/m2), primary fossil energy use, and renewable energy share. | energy-beng-standards |
| Biophilic design | An architectural approach that integrates natural elements -- daylight, plants, water, natural materials -- into the built environment to improve occupant well-being. | principles |
| Blower door test | A diagnostic test that pressurizes a building to 50 Pa to measure air leakage rate (n50 or q50). Required for Passive House certification and many national energy codes. | passive-house |
| Bouwbesluit (NL) | The Dutch Building Decree (Besluit bouwwerken leefomgeving since 2024 under Omgevingswet). Sets minimum technical requirements for structural safety, fire, energy, and health. | bouwbesluit-overview |
| Building envelope | The physical barrier between the conditioned interior and the exterior environment, comprising walls, roof, floor, windows, and doors. Its thermal performance is measured by U-values. | u-values |
| Cadastru (RO) / Kadaster (NL) | Land registration system that records property boundaries, ownership, and legal encumbrances. Required before obtaining a building permit in both Romania and the Netherlands. | land-registry-cadastre |
| Cantilever | A structural element anchored at one end only, projecting horizontally beyond its support. Common in modular construction for balconies and overhangs. | timber-frame |
| CE marking | European conformity mark required on construction products sold in the EU/EEA. Indicates compliance with harmonised European standards (hEN) or European Technical Assessments. | ce-marking-construction |
| CLT (Cross-Laminated Timber) | Engineered wood panel made of layers of lumber boards glued at right angles. High strength-to-weight ratio; used for walls, floors, and roofs in multi-storey timber buildings. | clt-timber |
| Condensation risk | The likelihood of moisture accumulating within or on building components when warm, humid air meets cold surfaces. Mitigated by correct vapor barrier placement and ventilation. | wall-buildup |
| CTE (ES) | Codigo Tecnico de la Edificacion -- Spain's main technical building code covering structural safety, fire, energy, acoustics, and accessibility. | |
| Dead load | The permanent static load of a structure, including self-weight of walls, floors, roof, and fixed installations. Calculated per Eurocode 1 (EN 1991). | eurocodes-overview |
| DIN standard | German national standard issued by Deutsches Institut fur Normung. Many DIN standards (e.g., DIN 4108 thermal protection) are harmonised with European EN standards. | |
| DPE (FR) | Diagnostic de Performance Energetique -- French energy performance certificate rating buildings from A to G. Mandatory for sale or rental of property. | energy-performance |
