# Residential Acoustics

## Overview
Good acoustics are invisible when done right — and unbearable when wrong. Sound insulation between rooms and from outside is essential for comfort and privacy. In modular construction, acoustic performance depends on wall composition, floor structure, and the quality of module-to-module joints.

## Sound Insulation Requirements

| Element | Romanian Standard | Dutch BBL | Recommended |
|---------|------------------|-----------|-------------|
| Party wall (between dwellings) | Rw ≥ 52 dB | DnT,A ≥ 52 dB | ≥ 55 dB |
| Internal wall (between rooms) | Rw ≥ 35 dB | — | ≥ 40 dB |
| Floor (between dwellings) | Rw ≥ 52 dB | Ln,T,A ≤ 54 dB (impact) | ≥ 55 dB airborne, ≤ 50 dB impact |
| External wall (from traffic) | Rw ≥ 35-45 dB | — | Depends on noise zone |
| Window (closed) | Rw ≥ 25-35 dB | — | Triple glazing: 35-38 dB |

## Sound Insulation by Wall Type

| Wall Construction | Thickness | Rw (dB) | Notes |
|-------------------|-----------|---------|-------|
| Single plasterboard on studs | 100mm | 35-38 | Minimal — bedroom dividers |
| Double plasterboard on studs + mineral wool | 125mm | 45-48 | Good internal wall |
| CLT 100mm + mineral wool + plasterboard | 175mm | 50-55 | Excellent — party wall |
| Double-leaf wall (separated studs) | 200mm+ | 55-62 | Best — between dwelling units |
| ModulCA module wall (exterior) | 265mm | 50-55 | External wall assembly |

## Impact Sound (Floors)

| Floor Construction | Impact Sound (Ln,w) | Notes |
|-------------------|-------------------|-------|
| CLT floor (bare) | 75-80 dB | Very poor — needs treatment |
| CLT + floating screed | 55-60 dB | Acceptable |
| CLT + resilient layer + screed | 45-50 dB | Good |
| CLT + floating floor on rubber pads | 40-45 dB | Excellent |

## Room Acoustics (Reverberation)

| Room | Target RT60 | Strategy |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Living room | 0.4-0.6s | Soft furnishings, curtains, rug |
| Bedroom | 0.3-0.5s | Bed, curtains, carpet/rug |
| Kitchen | 0.5-0.7s | Hard surfaces — add textile/cork accents |
| Home office | 0.3-0.5s | Acoustic panels, bookshelf, rug |
| Bathroom | 0.6-1.0s | Hardest room — add towels, bath mat |

## ModulCA Application
- **Module wall Rw**: Standard module exterior wall achieves 50-55 dB — exceeds internal requirements
- **Module-to-module seal**: EPDM gasket between modules provides acoustic break
- **Floor isolation**: Factory-install resilient layer under floating floor — impact sound controlled
- **CLT mass advantage**: CLT has better mass-law performance than lightweight stud walls
- **Acoustic upgrade option**: Double-leaf wall between bedroom and living modules for extra privacy
- **Bathroom isolation**: Bathroom module walls pre-lined with additional plasterboard layer
