# Interior Lighting Design

## Overview
Good lighting design layers three types of light — ambient, task, and accent — to create comfortable, functional, and atmospheric spaces. Natural light should be maximized during the day; artificial lighting should support circadian rhythms with warm, dimmable sources in the evening.

## Three Layers of Light

| Layer | Purpose | Examples | Control |
|-------|---------|---------|---------|
| Ambient (general) | Overall illumination | Ceiling pendants, downlights, indirect coves | Dimmable |
| Task | Focused work light | Desk lamp, under-cabinet, reading light | Switchable |
| Accent | Atmosphere, highlight | Picture lights, shelf lights, LED strip | Dimmable |

## Recommended Lux Levels

| Room / Activity | Lux (lm/m²) | Notes |
|----------------|-------------|-------|
| Living room (general) | 150-300 | Dimmable for evening |
| Living room (reading) | 300-500 | Task light at chair |
| Kitchen (general) | 300-500 | Even ambient |
| Kitchen (counter/task) | 500-750 | Under-cabinet LED |
| Bedroom (general) | 100-200 | Warm, low |
| Bedroom (reading) | 300-500 | Bedside task light |
| Bathroom (general) | 200-300 | Even, no shadows |
| Bathroom (mirror/task) | 400-600 | Vertical lighting at face |
| Home office | 500 | Even, minimize glare |
| Hallway / entrance | 100-200 | Welcoming, safe |
| Stairs | 150-300 | Safety, even coverage |

## Color Temperature Guide

| Kelvin | Name | Effect | Best For |
|--------|------|--------|----------|
| 2200-2700K | Warm white | Cozy, relaxing | Living room, bedroom, dining |
| 3000K | Warm neutral | Balanced warmth | Kitchen, bathroom, general |
| 3500-4000K | Neutral | Bright, alert | Home office, utility |
| 5000K+ | Cool/daylight | Clinical, energizing | Workshop, garage (avoid in home) |

## Room Lighting Schemes

| Room | Ambient | Task | Accent |
|------|---------|------|--------|
| Living room | Pendant or indirect cove | Floor lamp for reading | LED strip behind TV, art lights |
| Kitchen | Recessed downlights | Under-cabinet LED strip | Inside-cabinet lighting |
| Bedroom | Wall wash or pendant | Bedside reading light | Closet lighting, shelf lights |
| Bathroom | IP44 downlights | Mirror sidelights (vertical) | Niche/shelf LED strip |
| Home office | Panel or indirect light | Adjustable desk lamp | Monitor backlight |

## ModulCA Application
- **Factory pre-wired**: Each module wired for 3-layer lighting — ambient, task, and accent circuits
- **LED cove lighting**: Factory-install LED strip channel at ceiling-wall junction — instant ambient layer
- **CLT ceiling trick**: Indirect LED strip between CLT panels creates warm ceiling wash
- **Smart-ready**: Pre-wire for smart switches (Zigbee/Wi-Fi) — dimmable and color-tunable
- **Per-module circuits**: Each module on its own lighting circuit — independent control
- **Entrance lighting**: Motion-sensor exterior light + warm hallway pendant in entrance module
