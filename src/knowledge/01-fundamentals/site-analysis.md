# Site Analysis — Reading the Land

## Overview
Before designing, you must understand the site. Site analysis examines the physical, environmental, legal, and contextual characteristics of a plot of land to inform design decisions.

## Analysis Checklist

### Physical Characteristics

| Factor | What to Assess | Tool/Method |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| Topography | Slope, levels, drainage direction | Survey, contour map |
| Soil | Bearing capacity, water table, type | Geotechnical report (studiu geo) |
| Vegetation | Existing trees, protected species | Site visit, biology survey |
| Size & shape | Dimensions, area, irregular edges | Cadastral plan |
| Access | Road access, visibility, gradient | Site visit |
| Utilities | Water, sewer, electric, gas, telecom | CU (Certificat de Urbanism) |

### Environmental Factors

| Factor | What to Assess | Impact on Design |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Sun path** | Summer/winter arcs, overshadowing | Window placement, solar panels |
| **Wind** | Prevailing direction, speed, shelter | Cross-ventilation, wind protection |
| **Rainfall** | Annual, intensity, drainage patterns | Roof design, landscape drainage |
| **Noise** | Roads, railways, neighbors, industry | Room layout, sound barriers |
| **Views** | Positive (mountains, gardens) and negative | Window orientation, screening |
| **Microclimate** | Frost pockets, heat islands, shade | Plant selection, outdoor spaces |

### Legal & Planning

| Factor | What to Check | Source |
|--------|--------------|-------|
| Zoning | Land use, POT, CUT, height | PUG, Certificat de Urbanism |
| Setbacks | Front, side, rear distances | CU |
| Easements | Rights of way, utility corridors | Land registry |
| Protected status | Historic, environmental, Natura 2000 | Local authority |
| Flood risk | Flood maps, drainage capacity | County environmental agency |

### Context

| Factor | What to Consider |
|--------|-----------------|
| Neighboring buildings | Heights, styles, windows (privacy) |
| Street character | Rural, suburban, urban |
| Community | Schools, shops, transport nearby |
| Future development | Planned roads, buildings, infrastructure |

## Sun Path Analysis (Romania, 45°N latitude)

| Season | Sunrise | Noon Sun Altitude | Sunset |
|--------|---------|-------------------|--------|
| Summer solstice | NE (05:30) | 68° | NW (21:00) |
| Equinox | E (06:30) | 45° | W (18:30) |
| Winter solstice | SE (07:45) | 22° | SW (16:45) |

**Design implication**: South-facing windows receive sun year-round. Overhangs of ~0.6m on south windows block summer sun (68°) but admit winter sun (22°).
