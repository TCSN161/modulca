# Natural Materials in Architecture

## Overview
Natural materials create a direct sensory connection to nature — their textures, colors, scents, and warmth engage our biophilic instincts. Research shows that rooms with natural materials reduce stress, lower heart rate, and improve cognitive function compared to synthetic environments.

## Material Biophilic Properties

| Material | Visual | Tactile | Olfactory | Acoustic | Thermal |
|----------|--------|---------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Wood** | Warm grain patterns | Warm, textured | Cedar/pine scent | Absorptive | Warm to touch |
| **Stone** | Mineral patterns | Cool, solid | Minimal | Reflective | Cool to touch |
| **Clay/earth** | Earthy tones | Smooth/rough | Earthy | Absorptive | Temperature-regulating |
| **Wool** | Soft texture | Warm, soft | Subtle | Highly absorptive | Insulating |
| **Cork** | Organic pattern | Warm, resilient | Subtle | Absorptive | Warm to touch |
| **Bamboo** | Linear grain | Smooth | Mild | Moderate | Warm |
| **Linen/cotton** | Woven texture | Soft | Fresh | Absorptive | Breathable |

## Wood in Interiors — The Evidence

| Finding | Study/Source | Notes |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Reduced stress response | University of British Columbia | Exposed wood lowers sympathetic nervous system activity |
| Lower blood pressure | Austrian study (Holzforschung) | Solid wood rooms vs. synthetic finishes |
| Improved air quality | Wood breathes | Hygroscopic — absorbs/releases moisture |
| Warmer perception | Thermal conductivity 0.13 W/mK | Wood feels warm vs. concrete (1.7) or steel (50) |
| Preferred by occupants | Post-occupancy surveys | 80%+ prefer wood-finished interiors |

## Application by Room

| Room | Recommended Materials | Biophilic Effect |
|------|----------------------|------------------|
| Living room | Wood ceiling/accent wall, stone fireplace, wool textiles | Warmth, refuge |
| Bedroom | Wood headboard wall, linen bedding, wool carpet | Calm, comfort |
| Kitchen | Wood cabinetry, stone countertop, terracotta tiles | Earthiness, craft |
| Bathroom | Teak accents, stone basin, cork flooring | Spa-like calm |
| Home office | Wood desk, cork board, natural fiber rug | Focus, reduced stress |

## ModulCA Application
- **CLT exposed ceilings**: Leave CLT underside of roof module exposed — instant biophilic ceiling
- **Wood interior walls**: Plywood or timber cladding on module interior walls (pre-finished in factory)
- **Stone accent walls**: One module wall as natural stone veneer — visual anchor
- **Cork flooring**: Warm, resilient, acoustic — ideal for modular floors
- **Natural insulation**: Sheep wool or wood fiber insulation inside walls (invisible but healthier)
- **Exterior cladding**: Larch or thermowood facades — natural weathering (silver-grey patina)
