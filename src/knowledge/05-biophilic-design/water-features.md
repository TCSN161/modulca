# Water Features in Design

## Overview
Water is one of the most powerful biophilic elements. The sight and sound of water reduces stress, masks unwanted noise, increases humidity, and creates a dynamic sensory experience. Even small water features — a rain chain, a tabletop fountain, a birdbath — activate biophilic responses.

## Types of Residential Water Features

| Type | Indoor/Outdoor | Cost Range | Maintenance | Sound Level |
|------|---------------|------------|-------------|-------------|
| Rain chain | Outdoor (visible from inside) | €30-150 | Minimal | Gentle |
| Tabletop fountain | Indoor | €50-200 | Weekly (refill) | Quiet |
| Wall fountain | Indoor/outdoor | €200-1,000 | Monthly | Moderate |
| Reflecting pool | Outdoor | €500-3,000 | Bi-weekly | Silent |
| Rain garden | Outdoor | €200-1,000 | Seasonal | During rain |
| Bird bath | Outdoor | €50-300 | Weekly | Birds! |
| Natural pond | Outdoor | €1,000-5,000 | Seasonal | Variable |
| Rill/channel | Outdoor (courtyard) | €500-2,000 | Monthly | Gentle flow |

## Psychological Effects

| Effect | Mechanism | Application |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Stress reduction | Parasympathetic activation | Any visible/audible water |
| Focus improvement | Soft fascination (attention restoration) | Background water sounds |
| White noise masking | Broadband sound spectrum | Fountains near workspaces |
| Humidity regulation | Evaporative humidification | Indoor fountains in dry climates |
| Temperature perception | Evaporative cooling | Outdoor water in courtyards |

## Design Considerations

| Factor | Guideline | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Sound level | 40-50 dB at seating distance | Not louder than conversation |
| Water visibility | Visible from main living areas | Through window or in room |
| Safety | Shallow (<150mm) or covered | If children present |
| Hygiene | Circulating pump + UV/filter | Prevent stagnation |
| Energy | Solar-powered pump preferred | 5-15W for small features |
| Frost protection | Drain in winter (continental climate) | Or use frost-rated materials |

## ModulCA Application
- **Rain chains**: Replace conventional downpipes from module roof — visible from living room window
- **Courtyard water feature**: U-shape layout creates a natural courtyard for a small rill or reflecting pool
- **Rain garden**: Combine with waterschap requirements (NL) — retain first 20-60mm on plot
- **Module integration**: Pre-route water supply and drain for an indoor wall fountain
- **Terrace connection**: Water feature at module threshold — bridging indoor-outdoor
- **Sound design**: Position water feature near bedroom modules — natural sleep aid
