## One-sentence description

**ModulCA is a 14-step AI-powered design platform that turns anyone into an architect for modular, sustainable homes — from land selection to permit-ready drawings in under an hour.**

## The problem

- Modular construction in Europe grows 11% YoY but design bottleneck = architect fees (€3-8K per project, 2-6 week wait).
- First-time builders abandon 60% of projects at the "talk to an architect" step.
- Existing CAD tools (AutoCAD, Revit) need 100+ hours of training.
- No integrated platform from land → design → permit → build for Romanian/NL market.

## Our solution

A web-based platform with 14 guided steps that replaces a 40-hour architect engagement with 1 hour of self-service:

1. Choose or upload land → 2. Map & measure → 3. Drag-drop modular layout (3×3m grid) → 4-7. Style, configure, visualize in 3D → 8. AI-render photorealistic interiors/exteriors → 9. Auto-generate technical drawings → 10. VR walkthrough → 11. Furniture & product catalog → 12. Save/export → 13. Investor-ready PDF presentation → 14. Collaborate with professional architect (paid tier).
